Ted's Health

Brand Guidelines — April 2026

Mission

Maximising men's potential.

Modern, personalised treatments delivered straight to your door. No waiting rooms. No judgement. Just straightforward, doctor-led men's health.

Colour Palette

Navy Blue#1B365D

Teal#4A90A4
CTAs, links, accents
Warm Gold#D4A85C

White#FFFFFF

Off-White#F8F9FA

Typography

Montserrat Bold — Headlines
Montserrat SemiBold — Subheadings
Montserrat Regular — Body text. Clean, modern, geometric sans-serif. Used across all materials for consistency.

Weights

Regular (400) for body copy. SemiBold (600) for subheadings and emphasis. Bold (700) and ExtraBold (800) for headlines and hero text.

Logo

The Ted's Health logo is a clean wordmark in white, used primarily on dark backgrounds. Always maintain clear space around the logo equal to the height of the "T" in Ted's.

Logo Versions

  • Primary: White logo on dark navy (#1A1A2E) background
  • Reversed: Dark navy logo on white/light backgrounds
  • Never stretch, rotate, recolour, or add effects to the logo

Tone of Voice

Ted's Health speaks like a knowledgeable friend — not a doctor's waiting room, not a lads' mag. Warm, direct, and non-judgmental.

Do

"Check your levels — it takes 5 minutes"

"You deserve to feel like yourself again"

"Doctor-guided, from your sofa"

"Simple blood test, real answers"

Don't

"Suffering from low T? Act NOW!"

"Boost your testosterone FAST"

"Are you man enough?"

"Guaranteed results in 30 days"

Key Principles

  • Empathetic: Acknowledge the problem without shame
  • Direct: Say what it is, what it costs, how it works
  • Credible: Always reference real doctors, CQC, clinical process
  • Compliant: No cure claims. Use "may help", "could support", "if treatment is right for you"

Trust Signals — Always Include

Photography & Visual Style

Do Use

  • Real men in everyday settings — offices, kitchens, gyms, outdoors
  • Warm, natural lighting (morning light, golden hour)
  • Shallow depth of field, cinematic grade
  • Diverse ages 30-60, relatable not aspirational
  • Product shots: clean surfaces, warm tones, premium feel

Don't Use

  • Shirtless/bodybuilder imagery
  • Before/after body transformation photos
  • Sterile, cold clinical environments
  • Stock photos that look obviously staged
  • Aggressive or hyper-masculine imagery
Compliance Note: All Ted's Health advertising must comply with ASA/CAP codes for health services. No guarantee of results. All treatment is doctor-guided and based on individual blood work. Include "Results may vary" where appropriate.
