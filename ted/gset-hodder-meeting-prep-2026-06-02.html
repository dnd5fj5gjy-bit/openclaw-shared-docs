Likely questions

Studio booked, theologian locked, first Reading + Unpacking pair edited. Short-form testing loop running across three platforms. Substack — first essays drafted. Web build phase. Steady cadence kicks in Month 2.
"Is Bear doing any new filming?"
No. Pre-recorded only — audiobook, existing interviews, archive. His channels carry 3 anchor posts a year, written by the team, posted by him. That's deliberate.
"How will we know the US strategy is working?"
Geo-isolated velocity. US web sessions read against US retail velocity, month by month. Depends on monthly velocity reports from Hodder and our attribution tracker.
"How does AI fit in given Hachette policy?"
Backend only — transcription, scheduling, analytics. No AI in published voice, imagery or creative. Every editor signs the policy.
"What if the short-form formula doesn't land in Month 1?"
Asset bank is wide, matrix is wide — we'll see signal. If nothing clears the median we extend through Month 2 Week 1–2. The long-form schedule doesn't depend on it.
"What's the reporting cadence?"
Weekly ops check-in from Week 1. Monthly summary end of Month 1 with the format recommendation and the Month-2 slate. Quarterly strategy review starts end of Q1.
