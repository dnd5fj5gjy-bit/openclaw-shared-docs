Transparency

A Private Business — Not a White-Label Shell

Before we talk partnership, here is exactly what Ted's Health is and how the model works.

We're a live, private telehealth business

Ted's Health is a CQC-registered telehealth company with an active patient base already in our clinical pathway. We run our own direct-to-consumer men's health platform — prescribing doctors, clinical protocols, and fulfilment all live and operational. You're white-labelling a working clinical operation, not a concept.

Your registered patients enter our regulated clinical pathway

When a man is referred through your white-label, he becomes a patient in our CQC-registered clinical system — seen by our GMC doctors, prescribed through our protocols, and managed in our care pathway. You carry no clinical liability. We carry the medicine.

We operate alongside you — not instead of you

Ted's Health continues to run its own consumer brand in parallel. The white-label model extends our clinical infrastructure to partners — your brand, your client relationship, our clinical backend. You retain the trust; you earn on every active patient we manage.
