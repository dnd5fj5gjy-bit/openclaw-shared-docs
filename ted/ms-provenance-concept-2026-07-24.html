Slow, beautiful, quiet. Bison grazing across green mountain country, held long enough to breathe. One line of text, no hard sell. The calm counterpart to our punchy edits - this one builds the feeling of the brand and where real food begins.

01 / The idea

Simple, beautiful footage of bison grazing in lush green mountain country. Let the shots run long and calm - no fast cuts, no drama. One quiet line of text, and that is it. The point is to make people feel where real food should come from. Modern Savage is never sold on screen - it lives only in the caption, as the quiet answer.

02 / The one line

Simple, empowering, over the footage

One short line laid gently over the beautiful grazing shots. Aesthetic and empowering, never preachy - it should make the viewer feel something and want to choose better. Nothing else on screen. Options by tone below.

03 / Look & feel

Simple, slow, cinematic

  • Wide shot of bison grazing across a green mountain valley, mist and morning light
  • Slow drone drift over rolling green hills, herd small in the frame
  • Close, patient shot of a single bison grazing, breath and movement, shallow focus
  • Sunlight through long grass and greenery, dew, gentle wind
  • Wide landscape held still - mountains, green, sky - the animal small within it
Stock search terms
bison grazing green mountains buffalo herd valley aerial cattle grazing morning mist green rolling hills drone slow sunlight long grass wind

Pace: slow, long holds, minimal cuts. Grade: lush natural greens, soft warm light, nothing over-stylised. Let it look real and calm.

04 / Caption

Modern Savage as the quiet answer

The video stays pure and unbranded. The caption is where Modern Savage appears - as the solution the footage sets up, kept subtle, never salesy.

"Know where your food comes from. Whole-food nutrition, from the source. Modern Savage."

05 / Music

Calm and cinematic

  • Soft, emotive, ambient - let the visuals breathe
  • A gentle trending sound works if it fits the calm
  • No hard beat drop - this concept is stillness, not energy
  • Low and warm, never busy
