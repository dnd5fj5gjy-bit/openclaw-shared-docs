Type treatmentHeadline: DM Serif 48pt. Pill numbers/glyphs: DM Serif 20pt, gold. Pill labels: Montserrat caps 10.5pt, .18em tracking. Footer URL: monospaced (JetBrains Mono) 11pt — reinforces the technical register.

White-label removes that gate. You bring the brand and the audience; we bring the prescribers, the pharmacy, the blood-test partners, and the repeat-script logic that actually keeps a TRT or ED book retained. The reference deployment is rimo.co. The first external pilot is Effect Dr. If you've been quietly mapping out a men's-health platform of your own, this is the conversation.
