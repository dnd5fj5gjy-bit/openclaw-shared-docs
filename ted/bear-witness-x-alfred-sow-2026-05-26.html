What's included

A working layer behind your team.

This is a guidance, support and help engagement - not a full-service build. Alfred owns the roadmap, the calendar and every final call. Bear Witness gives you a senior strategy line and the editing horsepower to keep momentum where you need it most.

01 · Editing

24/7 editing support

Bear Witness's editing team on hand to turn Alfred's raw footage and source assets into finished, on-brand content. No per-project briefs needed - submit work as it arrives, get it back ready to ship. Effectively an always-on production layer behind your in-house team.

02 · Strategy

Content strategy consultation

Direct senior-level counsel on positioning, content direction, channel calls, format choices and campaign sequencing. We work integrated with Alfred's team and bring outside perspective when decisions get hard.

03 · Editorial

Editorial guidance

Review and shaping of in-progress work from the Alfred team - scripts, hooks, thumbnails, titles, sequencing, distribution plays. The kind of second pair of eyes that lifts the work from good to ready.

04 · Sessions

Working calls

A recurring weekly working call to keep things aligned, plus ad-hoc calls as priorities emerge. Direct line - no account managers, no layers.

05 · Review

Light-touch performance review

Periodic look at what's working and where to lean in, fed back into the next sprint of content.
