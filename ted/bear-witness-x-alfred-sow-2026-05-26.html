What's included

  • 24/7 editing support — Bear Witness's editing team available to turn Alfred's raw footage and source assets into finished, on-brand content; no per-project briefs needed, work submitted as it arrives.
  • Content strategy consultation — direct line to Bear Witness senior strategy on positioning, content direction, channel calls, format choices and campaign sequencing.
  • Editorial guidance — review and shaping of in-progress work from the Alfred team: scripts, hooks, thumbnails, titles, sequencing, distribution plays.
  • Working sessions — a recurring weekly working call plus ad-hoc calls as priorities emerge.
  • Light-touch performance review — periodic look at what's working and where to lean in, fed back into the next sprint of content.

What this is not

This SOW is help, not a full-stack build. It does not include paid media management, SEO content production, end-to-end campaign execution, influencer activation or full-time embedded resourcing. Those sit inside the wider Bear Witness × Alfred partnership proposal and can be added at any point.
