01 — The Problem

Good people run out of hours long before they run out of ideas.

Every business has employees who could do more — chase more leads, write more content, answer more customers, catch more admin — if there were simply more of them in the day. The usual answer is to hire, which is slow, expensive, and doesn't scale with a person's judgement, only their headcount.

What if instead of hiring more people, a business gave its existing people an agent — or a small team of agents — built specifically for their role? Not a generic chatbot bolted onto the business. A named, engineered agent that knows the job, does the repeatable parts of it, and reports back like a colleague would.

02 — Why Bear Witness

We're not describing this. We're running it.

Bear Witness already operates a live team of named AI agents, each built for a specific person and role inside the business — not a shared tool, a dedicated agent per person. That's the proof case: this isn't a concept slide, it's a working team.

Live example

A small team, coordinating

03 — The Product

One agent. Two agents. A whole engineered team.

The sale scales with the business, not against it. A sole trader might want one agent handling admin and customer replies. A growing team might want an agent per department head. A larger operation might want an entire coordinated agent workforce sitting alongside its human one.

Package 01

Single Agent

One engineered agent built for one role or person — the entry point. Proves the model fast, low commitment, immediate time-back.

Package 02

Team Agents

Two to five agents across a small team or department, coordinating with each other and reporting into one place.

Package 03

Full Workforce

An agent for every function that can carry one — the business's human team plus an engineered one running alongside it full-time.

04 — The Market

Not a tech buyer. Any business owner who's ever said "I need another pair of hands."

This doesn't require the client to understand AI, buy software, or manage a system — it requires them to describe a role they wish they could clone or afford to hire for. That's a founder question, not a technical one, which is what makes it sellable far outside BGV's own brand-safe circle: solo founders, small teams, professional services, anyone who's capped by their own hours.

05 — How It's Sold

Priced per agent. Simple enough to buy on a call.

The packaging mirrors headcount, which is a pricing model every business owner already understands intuitively — it's just cheaper, faster, and doesn't need holiday cover.

Single Agent
Build + monthly

One role, engineered and deployed. Fastest to sell, fastest to prove value, natural upsell path into more agents.

Team Agents
Build + monthly, tiered

Priced per agent with a coordination layer included once a client goes beyond one. Higher retainer, stickier relationship.

Full Workforce
Bespoke retainer

Whole-business engagement, priced against headcount genuinely displaced or augmented. Highest value, longest relationship.
