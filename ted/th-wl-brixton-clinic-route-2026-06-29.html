Brixton Day-Route
Clinic Pitch Guide

12 private clinics and wellness centres across South London, mapped into a single walkable + transit day. Start at Brixton tube.
Start: Brixton Tube SW2 12 Clinics 3 Clusters 29 June 2026
How to use this guide: Walk Cluster 1 from Brixton tube (north toward Oval). Bus or Victoria Line to Cluster 2 (Clapham North → Clapham Junction). Bus or Overground south to Cluster 3 (Herne Hill → Streatham).

Cluster 1
Brixton & Stockwell / Oval
On foot from Brixton tube — 0 to 20 min walk
Top Target
1
Macsoflondon Medical Aesthetics & Wellness
Piano House, 9 Brighton Terrace, Brixton SW9 8DJ 5 min walk from Brixton tube
Award-winning non-surgical aesthetics clinic and private wellness hub. Hosts Goodbody private blood testing on-site. Explicitly offers men's hormone health panels: testosterone levels, ED diagnostic screens, prostate markers, cholesterol, and hormone deficiency testing.
Why pitch TH WL here
They're already running testosterone and hormone diagnostics on male patients. The gap is what happens next — they identify low T but have nowhere to send men for management. TH WL closes that loop and adds a recurring revenue stream to every male patient they currently send home with a result and no follow-up.

Oval Medical Centre
2 Clapham Road, Stockwell SW9 0JG 15 min walk north / 3 min from Oval tube
Private and corporate GP clinic with men's health explicitly listed as a service area: erectile dysfunction, prostate check-ups, sexual health, and private blood testing. Mon–Fri 8:30am–7pm, Sat 8:30am–4pm. Accepts self-pay and all major insurers.
Why pitch TH WL here
A private GP already seeing men for ED and prostate concerns is sitting at the top of the TRT conversion funnel. TH WL gives them a complete men's hormone service to offer patients without building any clinical infrastructure — pure revenue uplift on an existing patient base.

Cluster 2
Clapham North, Common & Junction
Victoria Line (1 stop to Clapham North) + bus or walk west to Clapham Junction — 20–35 min from Brixton
Top Target
3
London Doctors Clinic — Clapham
Seven Lion Yard, Tremadoc Road, SW4 (near Clapham North tube) 20 min from Brixton / 2 min from Clapham North tube
Multi-location private GP group (19 London sites). This branch runs their Well Man Clinic: testosterone blood tests from £65 (4–6 hr results), male hormone profiles, sexual health testing, same-day appointments. Part of a national group — a yes here could unlock multiple sites.
Why pitch TH WL here
They actively test for testosterone deficiency but the group does not manage TRT in-house. Men who test low are currently referred out — TH WL turns that referral into a retained clinical service and a revenue share. Group scale also means a single partnership could extend across their network.

4
Virgin Active Clapham (Wellness & Spa)
4–20 North Street, Clapham SW4 0HG 5 min walk from Clapham Common tube
Premium health club with gym, 25m pool, spa, lounge, and fitness classes. High-end membership base. Ask for the Health & Wellbeing Manager or Club Manager — not reception. Do not pitch to a PT or class instructor.
Why pitch TH WL here
Virgin Active Clapham members are the core TH demographic — performance-focused men in their 30s–50s already investing seriously in their health. A TH WL partnership offers the club a premium clinical service for members with zero overhead, positioned as a member benefit rather than a commercial takeover.

My Healthcare Clinic — Clapham Junction

Bupa Health Centre — Clapham Junction

Private Bupa health assessments centre. Runs a "Well Health Men's Sexual Function Plan" covering erectile dysfunction, premature ejaculation, and low libido. Also provides comprehensive health assessments and targeted cancer screening for men. Corporate and self-pay.

SW11 Clinic — Aesthetic & Medical
45 Lavender Hill, Clapham Junction SW11 5QW 8 min walk from Clapham Junction / 5 min walk from Bupa
CQC-registered aesthetic medicine and medical clinic operated by NHS consultants. Botox from £175, dermal fillers, Profhilo, CO₂ laser, slimming treatments. Strong and growing male patient base. Has on-site medical consultation capability.

TH WL is the clinical offer that matches what these men actually want next.

sk:n Clinics — Clapham (Lavender Hill)

Herne Hill & Streatham
Train (London Overground/Thameslink) to Herne Hill or bus south from Brixton — 20–30 min
Top Target
9
Health Hub Medical Centre
282 Milkwood Road, Herne Hill SE24 0EZ 20 min from Brixton / 2 min walk from Herne Hill station
CQC-registered private medical centre. GP consultations, minor illness and injury, specialist referrals, podiatry, nutrition. Sean White Aesthetics (active male aesthetics practice) operates as a tenant in the same building — two pitches at one address. Accepts self-pay and most insurers except AXA PPP.
Why pitch TH WL here
Private medical centre plus active aesthetics practice under one roof is the ideal TH WL partner profile. Clinical governance is in place; male patient footfall is there; the aesthetics tenant brings additional men in. Pitch the WL as a premium men's health service that adds zero overhead and a meaningful revenue share to both businesses simultaneously.

Sean White Aesthetics
