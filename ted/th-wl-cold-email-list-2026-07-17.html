How to use this

Same compatibility rule as the call list applies, none of these currently offer TRT, ED treatment or HRT in-house. Use the white-label pitch (overview, biomarker pathway, in-person bloods/injections point, incremental revenue, CQC-owned clinical risk) adapted per business type. Aesthetic clinics and GPs are the strongest fits, they already have a patient base and a physical space to build on.

Confidence tag: HIGH = email pulled directly from the business's own official contact/footer page. NONE = no email published anywhere on site, contact form or phone only.
