### Trustpilot
- **Score:** 4.0 / 5.0 stars
- **Total reviews:** 2,756
- **URL:** trustpilot.com/review/dirteaworld.com

**Top 3 Positive Review Themes:**
1. **Product quality** — "smooth taste", "superior consistency", mushroom coffee and cacao praised most
2. **Customer service** — described as "kind, generous, professional", quick response times

3. **Price** — products seen as expensive (~£1.60/mug for cacao, £38+ for coffee)
