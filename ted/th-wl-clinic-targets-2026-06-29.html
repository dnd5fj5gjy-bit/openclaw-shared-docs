1

Private GP Clinics

Already serving male patients. Test for hormones but refer TRT out — losing the patient and the revenue.

The WellMan Clinic
Weymouth Street, London W1 · wellmanclinic.org
Fit
Why a good fit

40+ years as a dedicated men's clinic. High-net-worth male clientele. Already doing advanced hormonal blood testing. Owner-operated — decision maker is in the building.

Pitch angle

"You're already doing the clinical work. Ted's Health standardises, scales, and tech-enables your TRT offering without you building the infrastructure yourself."

Harley Street Health Centre
Harley Street, London W1 · harleyhealthcentre.com
Fit
Why a good fit

Independent GP, Well Man packages, same-day walk-in model. Operating since 1998. Broad male patient base. Well Man package naturally leads to hormone conversations.

Pitch angle

"Your Well Man results flag low T — then what? White-label Ted's Health and convert that consultation into a recurring care revenue line."

Summerhill Health
Manchester & Cheshire · summerhillhealth.co.uk
Fit
Why a good fit

Private men's health checks, same-day appointments, Manchester and Cheshire catchment where private men's health provision is thinner. No TRT programme visible.

Pitch angle

"Go from 'we test hormones' to 'we treat and manage testosterone health' — without building it yourselves." Northern market opportunity.

The Doctors Practice
Edgbaston, Birmingham · thedoctorspractice.co.uk
Fit
Why a good fit

CQC-regulated, doctor-led, already covers hormone levels and TRT conversations. Edgbaston is an affluent catchment. Independent — owner accessible.

Pitch angle

"You're already having hormone conversations. Make them into a structured, branded, scalable men's health programme." Birmingham day-trip candidate.

Mayfield Clinic
Edinburgh · mayfieldclinic.co.uk
Fit
Why a good fit

Men's health check-ups, testosterone blood tests, sexual health in Edinburgh — a market with few specialist TRT providers. Independent clinic.

Pitch angle

"Scotland is underserved for private men's health. White-label Ted's Health and be first to market with a comprehensive programme under your own brand."

Community-Led Health Businesses

Gyms, pharmacies, wellness centres with the client base but not the clinical infrastructure. Ted's Health gives them a credible men's health service under their own name.

Third Space
15 clubs across London · thirdspace.london
Fit
Why a good fit

Premium London health club (£245–325/month). Already operates in-club sports medicine clinics. High-earning male members 30–55 — exactly who buys TRT. 15 clubs, expanding. Performance and longevity brand.

Pitch angle

"White-label a Third Space Health men's hormone programme — blood testing + TRT management — sitting inside your existing clinical services. Keeps revenue in-house, differentiates vs. Equinox."

Fitness Worx Gyms
UK (multiple sites) · fitnessworx.co.uk
Fit
Why a good fit

Already introduced private blood testing for members — including testosterone and hormonal health. They've broken the "gyms don't do clinical" barrier themselves. Just needs the next step.

Pitch angle

"You've already done the hard bit — members accept blood testing. White-label Ted's Health as the complete TRT pathway downstream of your testing. Member flags low T → you manage treatment under your brand."

Goodbody Clinic
40+ UK clinics · goodbodyclinic.com
Fit
Why a good fit

Private blood testing network, 40+ UK clinics. Already offers a 67-biomarker Men's Well Man test with hormone, cardiovascular, PSA markers. They test but don't treat — TH is the missing piece. Massive distribution.

Pitch angle

"You produce a lot of positive testosterone results. Right now those patients leave and find treatment elsewhere. White-label Ted's Health to close the loop — testing to managed treatment — without building clinical ops."

Manchester Pharmacy & Health Clinic
Manchester · manchesterpharmacy.co.uk
Fit
Why a good fit

Pharmacist-led TRT service already operating. Experienced prescribing pharmacist, on-site blood testing. Building something organically — resource-constrained. Good Manchester anchor for a day-trip.

Pitch angle

"You've got the clinical capability. Ted's Health gives you a premium product identity and the platform to expand beyond Manchester without building the tech stack yourself."

Athlete Lab UK
London · athletelab.uk
Fit
Why a good fit

Performance testing lab — VO2 max, lactate, blood tests, nutrition. Deep in biomarker testing already. Client base skews competitive male, willing to invest in physiology. Testosterone is a natural adjacent marker.
