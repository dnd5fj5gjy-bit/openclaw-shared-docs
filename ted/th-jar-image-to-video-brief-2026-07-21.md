## Objective
Animate this still into a 6-10 second looping/standalone video clip. This is a **placeholder** for weight-loss ad creative — needs to look premium and finished, but doesn't need to be the final hero video (a live-action or 3D shoot can replace it later).

## Deliverable specs
- **Length:** 6-10 seconds, loopable (start and end frames should match or blend cleanly)
- **Formats:** 9:16 (Reels/Stories/TikTok) and 1:1 (feed) — two exports from the same animation
- **Resolution:** 1080x1920 (9:16) and 1080x1080 (1:1), H.264 mp4
- **No text/copy overlay** — clean product motion only, text will be added at ad-build stage

## Creative direction
- Subtle camera movement (slow push-in or gentle parallax/dolly) — not a static image with a Ken Burns zoom that looks cheap
- Let the light do the work: soft dappled shadow movement across the wall (as if from moving foliage/blinds) sells "premium morning routine" without needing new elements
- Optional: very light steam/water ripple in the glass, or a subtle focus pull between the jar and the tablet, if achievable without looking AI-generated or gimmicky
- Keep the jar sharp and in focus throughout — it's the hero
- No added graphics, no logo animation, no confetti/sparkle effects — restrained and editorial, matching TH's existing brand tone

## What to avoid
- No obvious AI-video artifacts (warping label, morphing tablet, flickering shadows)
- No fast/punchy motion — this is a calm, aspirational wellness tone, not high-energy
- No added on-screen text or captions
