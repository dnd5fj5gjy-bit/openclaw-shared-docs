On the day — how to work it

Cold walk-in approach

Ask for the person by role, not name. "Is the Practice Manager or GM around for 5 minutes?" Keep it brief. Leave the booklet even if they're busy — that's the whole point of having it.

What to say at the door

"I'm Olly from Ted's Health — part of Bear Grylls Ventures. We run a white-label men's hormone health programme for clinics and gyms. I've got a one-page booklet — is there someone I could leave it with and book a 20-minute call?"

Log every stop

Note who you spoke to and what they said — even "they were busy" is useful. Send a follow-up email same day. Ted can draft them in bulk from your notes.

QR code is your follow-up

Even if they only glance at the booklet and scan the QR, they're in the funnel. The partner platform page does the pitch work after you've left.
