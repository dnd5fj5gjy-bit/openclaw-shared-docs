How to Read the Scripts

HOOK First 1-3 seconds. Bold text + scroll-stopping visual. No VO yet.
YOUR VO What you record. Italic = exact script. Grey = delivery direction.
STOCK / B-ROLL Premium stock footage. Search terms in green tags.
PRODUCT Modern Savage product footage you shoot yourself.
TEXT OVERLAY On-screen text. Animated or static.
CTA Closing frame with branding and call to action.

Premium Visual Standard

Every ad in this campaign must feel cinematic. Use Artgrid or Storyblocks Pro over Pexels. Prioritise footage with shallow depth of field, deliberate composition, and cinematic colour grading. Natural light but beautiful natural light: golden hour, soft directional morning light, not flat overhead. Every shot should feel pulled from a documentary, not a stock library. Slow, intentional camera movement (slow push-ins, gentle tracking) over static locked-off shots. Your product footage must match this quality: shoot on the best lens you have access to, shallow depth of field, clean surfaces, warm tones.

VO Recording Notes

Record in a quiet room. Phone voice memo works for testing; use a decent mic for finals. Natural conversational pace, not presenter-voice. Like explaining something to a friend over coffee. Warm, direct, grounded. Leave 1-2 second pauses between sections for clean edit points. Record each ad as one continuous take, then record each section individually as backup coverage.

Product Footage You Need to Shoot

You can cover every ad in this brief with one product shoot. Shot list: the Adult Blend bag on a clean counter in natural morning light; the Kids Blend bag beside it; a scoop going into the powder; powder tipping into a glass; water being poured in and stirred; the mixed drink on a counter; both bags side by side; close-up of the ingredient label; close-up of the nutrition panel; the bag in a kitchen setting with everyday items around it. Shoot in natural morning light. Get both vertical (9:16) and horizontal (16:9) angles for every shot. Treat it like premium brand photography, not product demo.
