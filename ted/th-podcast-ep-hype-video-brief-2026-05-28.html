Episode 4 Launch — Hype Video

A tight vertical hype ad to drive the Episode 4 launch across IG Reels, TikTok and YouTube Shorts. Shot-by-shot below — built to cut straight from the recorded episode footage and b-roll.

Format  9:16 vertical

Captions  Burned-in, always
KU Task  ref #192

Objective & tone

Single job: make someone stop scrolling and tap "listen". Lead with the most interesting moment, not a logo.

  • Tone: credible, clinical, calm-confident. Dr Jonathan Andrews is the trusted voice — let his delivery carry it.
  • Hook in first 2 seconds. Open on the strongest spoken line or question of the episode, not on branding.
  • Plain language. No hype metaphors, no "this will change your life", no health overclaiming. State what the episode covers, accurately.
  • Compliance: no specific medical promises or dosage claims on screen. Keep statements general and educational.
Episode specifics are placeholders. The episode topic and any guest were not yet documented at brief time. Production notes

Music & sound

  • Understated, modern, credible — light percussive bed with forward momentum. Not EDM, not dramatic-trailer.
  • Duck music under speech so every word is clear.
  • Subtle whoosh/cut SFX on transitions, used sparingly.

Captions & type

  • Burned-in captions throughout (most views are muted).
  • Montserrat for kickers/end-card; clean sans for captions.
  • Accent colour gold #C9A14A; base navy #0B1F3A — match the launch graphic.
  • Keep type inside safe zones — clear of IG/TikTok UI top & bottom.

Deliverables from KU

  • Master: 9:16, 15–25s, captions burned in, end-card branded.
  • Optional 6s cut-down for paid/teaser use (Beat 1 + end-card only).
  • Clean end-card still exported as a frame for thumbnail use.
  • Pair with the static launch graphic (square) for the feed post.
