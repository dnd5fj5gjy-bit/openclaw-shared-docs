Context - the one thing to build around

Bear-and-wild, not farm-and-gentle

Our main competitor sits in a farm-and-gentle lane - local farmers, pastoral tone, soft aesthetic. Our lane is Bear-and-wild: a globally known adventurer and his whole family, plus savage, nose-to-tail, expedition DNA that no farm-led brand can fake.

So we do not borrow the soft farm look. Everything below leans rugged, real and honest. That is the edge, and every Highlight should reinforce it.

01 / Fix the bio first

Add the one thing it is missing

The bio is solid but has no call to action - and pre-launch, that is its only job. Every profile visit should have a way in.

Drop "the ultimate" - it reads like every other brand. The tagline and the family do the work. The new line adds the strapline, keeps the founder credit, and points every visitor at the waitlist.

02 / The seven Highlights

Build these, in this order

Seven Highlights, each one distinctly ours. "What goes in it" is for whoever builds the covers and content. "Why it is ours" is the reason it earns a slot.

01

Our Family Anchor

Goes in itThe founder story - Bear, Shara and the three boys, and why they built Modern Savage. Real voice, real footage, the origin.
Why it is oursThe biggest edge we have. A globally famous adventurer and his whole family behind the brand is something no farm-led competitor can fake. Lead with it.
02

Nothing Hidden

Goes in itThe full label. Every ingredient, where it is sourced, the nose-to-tail thinking, nothing synthetic. Show the actual blend.
Why it is oursTransparency as proof. Show what is in it - do not claim what it does, which also keeps us clear of advertising-standards trouble. This is the trust builder.
03

The Blends

Goes in itThe range explained - Modern Savage, Mini Savage and Summit Stack, and what each one is for.
Why it is oursWe have a range, not a single tub. Lay the three out clearly so people see the system, not one product.
04

Mini Savage

Goes in itThe kids' blend as its own story - why an animal-based blend for children, and how the family uses it.
Why it is oursA dedicated children's product is a real differentiator. Competitors talk about "every generation" - we have an actual product for the youngest one. Own it.
05

Founding Tribe

Goes in itHow to get in first - the waitlist, what founding members get, and how to join.
Why it is oursThe main conversion point right now. This is where the new bio CTA lands, and where pre-launch attention turns into a list.
06

Wild

Goes in itThe Bear adventure and ancestral-eating angle - expedition, survival, and eating the way humans were built to.
Why it is oursRugged and real. Wild country, not farm gardens. This is the aesthetic lane that is uniquely ours and the one to protect hardest.
07

The Build

Goes in itBehind the scenes of a brand that has not launched yet - honest progress, the making of, the road to launch.
Why it is oursTurns "nothing to sell yet" into a story. Builds anticipation and fills the pre-launch gap with something genuinely authentic.
03 / Hold the line

Across every cover and every clip: rugged, real, honest. Dark, wild, expedition-grade visuals - never soft-farm pastel. Show, do not claim. If a Highlight could sit just as easily on a gentle farm brand's profile, it is not Modern Savage yet.
