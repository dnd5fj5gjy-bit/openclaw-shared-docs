Campaign Architecture

Ted's Health is a CQC-registered, doctor-led online men's health clinic specialising in testosterone replacement therapy (TRT) and erectile dysfunction (ED) treatment. Every ad in this campaign is built from premium stock footage, voiceover, and clean on-screen text. No patient faces. No before-and-after transformations. The tone is warm, factual, and destigmatising. We are talking to men who feel something is wrong but have been told it's "just getting older."

Top of Funnel

Problem Aware

Make men recognise their symptoms. No product, no brand. Just an honest mirror held up to what they have been normalising: chronic tiredness, brain fog, weight gain, low libido. The goal is recognition, not conversion.

Objective: Video views / reach
Audience: Broad interests (men's health, fitness, over-35, wellness, self-improvement)

Mid Funnel

Solution Aware

Introduce Ted's Health as a solution. Explain how TRT diagnosis works, the price point, and why doctor-led matters. Demystify the process. Remove the fear of the unknown.

Objective: Traffic / engagement
Audience: Retarget TOF viewers + lookalikes

Bottom of Funnel

Trust + Convert

CQC credentials, real doctors, Bear Grylls Ventures backing. Permission to act. Direct, simple CTA to book. These run to men who have already engaged with the brand.

Objective: Conversions
Audience: Retarget MOF engagers + site visitors

How to Read the Scripts

HOOK First 1-3 seconds. Bold text + scroll-stopping visual. No VO yet.
VO Voiceover script. Italic = exact script. Grey = delivery direction.
STOCK / B-ROLL Premium stock footage. Search terms in green tags.
PRODUCT Product or brand footage (website, blood test kit, app).
TEXT OVERLAY On-screen text. Animated or static.
CTA Closing frame with branding and call to action.

Compliance & Tone Guidelines

Ted's Health is a medical service. Every ad must comply with ASA/CAP and Meta advertising policies for health services. Critical rules:

1. Never make cure claims. Use "may help", "could support", "designed to support" language.

2. Never guarantee outcomes. Individual results vary.

3. Always position as doctor-led and medically supervised.

4. Do not use before/after imagery or imply dramatic transformations.

5. The tone is empathetic and destigmatising, never shaming or fear-based.

6. Avoid explicit sexual content. ED references should be tasteful and indirect.

Premium Visual Standard

Every ad must feel cinematic and premium. Use Artgrid or Storyblocks Pro. Prioritise footage with shallow depth of field, deliberate composition, and cinematic colour grading. Natural light: golden hour mornings, soft directional light through windows. Men in their 30s-50s in real-life settings: offices, kitchens, gyms, family moments. The visual world should feel aspirational but attainable. Avoid sterile clinical settings. When showing medical/consultation elements, keep them warm and human. Slow, intentional camera movement (slow push-ins, gentle tracking) over static locked-off shots.

VO Recording Notes

The voiceover should be a warm male voice. Mid-range, grounded, empathetic. Think: a trusted friend who also happens to be a doctor. Not a broadcaster. Not a fitness influencer. Natural conversational pace with quiet confidence. The tone says "I understand, and there is something you can do about this." Leave 1-2 second pauses between sections for clean edit points. Record each ad as one continuous take, then record each section individually as backup coverage.

Brand Footage & Assets Needed

Ted's Health blood test kit (unboxing, kit on a kitchen counter, the packaging). The tedshealth.com website on a phone and laptop screen (homepage, booking page, dashboard). If available: Dr. Johnny in a consultation setting (warm office, not sterile clinic). Ted's Health logo and brand elements for end cards. All brand footage should match the cinematic warmth of the stock footage. Shoot in natural light, clean surfaces, warm tones.

Delivery Notes for Ku

Format & Specs

All ads should be delivered in two formats:

9:16 (Reels / Stories) — Primary format. All 8 ads.

1:1 (In-Feed) — Secondary format for Ads 4, 6, 7.

Resolution: 1080x1920 (9:16) / 1080x1080 (1:1). Export: H.264, high bitrate.

Music & Sound Design

Subtle, warm underscore throughout. Think: ambient piano, soft pads, light percussion. The music supports the VO, never competes with it. TOF ads should feel slightly more reflective and melancholic. MOF ads: cleaner, more forward-moving. BOF ads: confident, resolved. Royalty-free from Artlist or Epidemic Sound. No EDM. No corporate ukulele. The vibe is premium documentary.

Typography & Branding

On-screen text should use Ted's Health brand fonts where available, or a clean sans-serif (Inter, DM Sans, or similar). Navy (#1B365D) and white as primary text colours. Gold (#D4A85C) for accents and highlights only. Teal (#4A90A4) for secondary elements. All end cards: Ted's Health logo centred, URL below, dark background. Keep it clean and premium. No busy graphics, no emoji, no exclamation marks.

Subtitles

All ads must have burned-in subtitles. Clean, white, bottom-third positioning. No auto-generated captions. Manually timed to the VO. Use a subtle dark drop-shadow or semi-transparent background bar for legibility over light footage. Subtitle text should match the VO script exactly.
