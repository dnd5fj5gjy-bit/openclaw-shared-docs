Below is the full breakdown on the Organised Nutrition situation for your meeting.  Lay it out as is or hand it over.  All of the facts and evidence we are relying on are captured here.

## 1. The Deal

- Endorsement and equity partnership between Bear Grylls and Organised Nutrition (founder: Niall Kiddle).
- Heads of Terms (HOT) executed.  The structure was: Bear provides endorsement, name, image, and content approval rights.  In return, Bear receives an agreed equity stake.
- The deal was treated by both sides as binding while the long form was being papered.  Bear performed.  Equity was not issued.

## 2. What Niall / Organised Did

- Used Bear's name, image, and endorsement publicly across their website, LinkedIn, email marketing, and paid social.
- Meta Ad Library shows 2,105 ads scraped from Organised's accounts, of which 84 are confirmed Bear Grylls NIL ads running November 2025 through February 2026.
- A Forbes piece in January 2026 referenced the partnership.
- Bear posted in support on his own channels during the same period.
- Organised continued operating off the back of Bear's endorsement while never issuing the equity contemplated by the HOT.

## 3. The Approvals Question

- Day-to-day content approvals were handled informally by Jesse via WhatsApp and direct email with Niall.
- All approvals were given on a goodwill basis while we expected the HOT to be formalized.  No unconditional license was ever issued.
- There were clear instances where Bear's name and image were used without Jesse's explicit sign-off (website, LinkedIn posts, general email marketing).  These can be identified specifically if needed.
- Position: any approvals must be read as conditional on the HOT completing.  Once Niall walked away from the HOT, the basis for those approvals fell away.

## 4. Niall's Termination and the Mishcon Letter

- Niall terminated the relationship.
- Mishcon de Reya wrote to us on his behalf [MDR ref: MISHCON_LIVE.FID2501656].
- The thrust of their position appears to be twofold: (a) push back on any continuing equity claim, and (b) raise a confidential information / IP angle around Modern Savage to create leverage.
- Ignition Law (Tammy Evans, Partner, supported by Paolo Andreottola on IP) is representing us.  Tammy circulated a draft response letter for review on May 18.

## 5. Our Position and Desired Outcome

- We want the equity.  Full stop.  The HOT was a binding commitment.  We are seeking specific performance, not a monetary settlement.
- We also want clean, permanent confirmation that Organised has ceased all use of Bear's name and image.
- We are not interested in being bought off cheaply.  Bear's endorsement generated real, traceable commercial value for Organised over the relationship.

## 6. Modern Savage — Our Defense

This is the area Mishcons is trying to attack us on.  Three killer facts:

1. **Companies House filing predates everything.**  The original entity behind what became Modern Savage was filed in 2007, more than a decade and a half before any contact with Niall or Organised.
2. **Product development predates the HOT by 15 months.**  A May 2024 email chain shows active product development on Modern Savage well before the Organised HOT was on the table.
3. **The benchmark was a US competitor, not Organised.**  When Bear referenced a formula comparison in his March 2026 email, the comparison was against Paul Saladino's Lineage Provisions, a publicly available US competitor in the ancestral nutrition / organ meat category.  Not Organised's confidential materials.

Wider points:

- Modern Savage sits in a different product category (organ meat / ancestral nutrition supplements — heart, liver, kidney).  The category is well established with multiple incumbents (Ancestral Supplements, Heart and Soil, Lineage Provisions, and others).
- The concept came from Bear's personal interest in ancestral nutrition and independent research, not from anything Niall shared.
- During the active relationship Jesse received some marketing decks and high-level product info from Niall via WhatsApp and email, all in the context of content approvals.  None of it informed Modern Savage.
- Modern Savage is, if anything, evidence of our good faith in the Organised deal.  It is the same conviction in the health supplement space that made us want to partner with them in the first place.  Niall is the one who walked.
