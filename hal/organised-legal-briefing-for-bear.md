## 3. Our Position

a) **Equity remedy.**  HOT was binding.  NK never issued the Securities.  We are pursuing specific performance, not damages.

b) **Approvals were conditional.**  All consent to use BG IP was given on a goodwill basis while we expected the HOT to be formalized into the Option Agreement.  No formal licence was ever executed.  Once NK walked, the basis for those approvals fell away.

c) **The Option Agreement was a negotiating draft, not a fait accompli.**  Draft commercial terms get traded.  Sending a first draft with protective terms is not strong-arming;  it is how this work is done.

d) **Phil Murray (TC Group) emailed CJ on 4 March 2026 saying NK could not proceed.**  That is NK's abandonment, not ours, and it predates the Modern Savage launch.

e) **Modern Savage is independent.**
   - The corporate vehicle that became Modern Savage was filed at Companies House in 2007, more than a decade before NK or Organised entered the picture.
   - Product development was active by May 2024, fifteen months before the HOT.
   - The formula benchmark in Bear's March 2026 internal email was against Paul Saladino's Lineage Provisions, a public US competitor in the ancestral organ-meat category.  Not Organised.
   - The bovine organ category has multiple established incumbents (Ancestral Supplements, Heart and Soil, Lineage Provisions and others).  Ingredient overlap is endemic to the category.

f) **Evidence we hold.**
