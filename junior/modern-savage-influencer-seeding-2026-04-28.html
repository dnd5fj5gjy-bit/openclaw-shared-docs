The Strategy

Modern Savage is not a mainstream supplement. The organ blend story requires credible voices who already believe in ancestral/primal nutrition — not celebrity endorsements, but authentic practitioners who will try it and speak genuinely.

The ask: Gifting only. No paid partnership, no scripts. Send the product, a brief handwritten-style letter, and let them post or not post as they choose. No obligation framing. The more genuine the reaction, the more useful it is.

The mechanism: Ship 3-bottle pack (1-month supply) with a brief letter from Bear. Target: arrive June 1-7. Brief: post if you love it, no pressure if not. Aim for 6-10 organic posts by June 25, ahead of July 1 launch.

The "Founding Savage" Package (What to Send)

1. 3x Modern Savage bottles (1-month supply, adult organ blend)
2. Handwritten-style letter from Bear (short — see template below)
3. One-page product brief — what's in it, why organ nutrition, Bear's personal ritual
4. No paid deal language, no disclosure requirement (gifting is gifting — they disclose if they post and they see fit)
