## What Email 2 Must Do

Email 1 told the story of why Modern Savage exists. The reader knows the origin. Now they need proof — not claims, facts. The supplement industry has earned distrust through exaggerated promises. The antidote is specificity. Specific ingredients, specific mechanisms, specific sources. No hyperbole.

Email 2 is the credibility email. It answers the question every intelligent buyer is asking: "This sounds good, but is it actually backed up?"

The psychology here: the reader replied "yes" (or didn't reply but opened). They've invested a small amount of cognitive commitment. Email 2 should reward that investment by treating them like an adult. Give them the real science, not a watered-down version. People who care enough about their health to seek out an organ supplement blend want to understand *why* it works.

**Segment note:** People who replied "yes" to Email 1 are your highest-intent cohort. Consider sending them a slightly warmer version with a P.S. that acknowledges their reply.
