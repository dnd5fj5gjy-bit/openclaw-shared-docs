# Modern Savage — Survival Academy Distribution Channel
**Prepared:** April 26, 2026
**Author:** Junior
**Purpose:** An under-exploited zero-cost distribution channel hiding inside Bear's own ecosystem.

---

## The Opportunity

Bear Grylls Survival Academy runs courses across UK locations (Cowdray Estate and others). ~$2M revenue, 34 staff, corporate team building plus individual courses.

The people who pay to attend a Bear Grylls Survival Academy are:
- Bear fans who trust him enough to book an experience
- Health and performance oriented (they're spending a weekend doing outdoor challenges)
- Predominantly men 25-55
- Already in a buying mindset around Bear-branded products

This is the Modern Savage target buyer, physically in a Bear Grylls environment, having just had a positive emotional experience with the brand. There is no better moment to introduce Modern Savage.

---

## The Mechanic (Simple)

**Option 1 — Sample sachet in participant pack (lowest friction)**
Every Survival Academy participant gets a single-serve sachet of Modern Savage in their welcome pack, with a QR code to the waitlist/shop and a "founding member" discount code. Cost: ~£0.50 per participant for sachet + print. No commercial negotiation needed if both brands are Bear's.

**Option 2 — QR code at course locations**
Table card or sticker at check-in: "Bear drinks this. Now you've done what Bear does." Links to modernsavage.co. No samples needed, pure interest capture.

**Option 3 — Survival Academy Bundle**
A landing page offer: buy a 3-month Modern Savage subscription, get 20% off a Survival Academy booking. Drives both brands simultaneously. Needs website integration but minimal tech.

**Option 4 — Instructor seeding**
The 34 Survival Academy staff are authentic Bear Grylls advocates. They're not Instagram influencers but they have real credibility with customers. Seed them with 2-month supply before launch. Their genuine use creates organic word-of-mouth in the communities they operate in — corporate wellness, outdoor sports, military veterans.

---

## The Numbers

Rough estimate of Survival Academy participants per year:
- At $2M revenue with an average course cost of £150-300/person: ~7,000-13,000 participants/year
- If 10% convert to Modern Savage trial from a sample: 700-1,300 new leads at ~£0.50 each in sampling cost
- If 5% of those subscribe at £69/month: 35-65 new customers with £591 LTV each = £20,685-38,415 in customer LTV
- Zero paid CAC. The distribution channel already exists.

This is the highest-ROI distribution channel Modern Savage has outside of Bear's social media.

---

## What Makes This Easy

Both brands are Bear's. The Survival Academy isn't an external partner that needs persuading — it's part of the same ecosystem. The conversation is internal: "Can we put a Modern Savage sachet in every participant pack for the next 12 months?"

The Academy operationally has 34 staff and existing supplier relationships. Adding a sachet to a participant pack is a 20-minute conversation with whoever runs operations there.
