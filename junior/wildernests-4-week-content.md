# Wildernests x Bear Witness — 4-Week Social Content Plan

Platform: Instagram + TikTok (Reels-first, with static backup)
Voice: Bear Grylls — adventure, freedom, building something different
Goal: Awareness + pre-launch demand for homes launching May 2026

---

## WEEK 1 — WHAT IS THIS?
*Objective: Curiosity and intrigue. Don't explain everything. Make people lean in.*

**Post 1 — Hook reel (15-30s)**
Visual: Aerial of wild land. A modular structure appearing. Time-lapse build.
Caption: "What if you could own land anywhere in the world — and have your home ready in weeks, not years. This is what we're building. @wildernests"
CTA: "Follow to see what's coming."

**Post 2 — Bear voiceover / talking head**
Visual: Bear outdoors — mountain, forest, somewhere wild.
Script: "I've spent my life sleeping in places most people never will. But I've always believed everyone deserves a home that feels like that. Off-grid. Yours. Built fast. That's Wildernests."
Caption: "The grid was never designed for people like us. @wildernests"

**Post 3 — Visual teaser (static or carousel)**
Visual: 3 renders of the Wildernests structure in different environments — mountain, desert, coast.
Caption: "Same home. Three continents. Your land. Your rules."

---

## WEEK 2 — WHY IT MATTERS
*Objective: Connect emotionally to the value props. One post per core problem solved.*

**Post 4 — Freedom angle**
Visual: Time-lapse of traditional planning permission process vs Wildernests build speed.
Caption: "The average planning permission in the UK takes 2+ years. We've found a better way. Build in weeks. Own forever. @wildernests"

**Post 5 — Investment angle**
Visual: Map showing land prices in remote areas vs cities.
Caption: "Raw land in the right place is still one of the best assets you can buy. Develop it fast and you've changed the equation completely. @wildernests"

**Post 6 — Sustainability angle**
Visual: Interior of a Wildernests home. Clean lines, natural light, off-grid systems.
Caption: "Next generation construction. Zero compromise on the environment. And honestly — it looks better than anything I've seen built the traditional way. @wildernests"

## WEEK 3 — SOCIAL PROOF + ASPIRATION
*Objective: Make it real. Show real people, real locations, real momentum.*

**Post 7 — Founder story (Alex Aboshiha)**
Visual: Alex on site, or a Q+A reel with Bear asking him questions.
Caption: "I asked Alex why he started Wildernests. The answer is exactly the kind of thinking that made me want to back this. @wildernests"

**Post 8 — Community angle**
Visual: Multiple Wildernests plots across different landscapes, montage.
Caption: "60 families. 12 countries. All building something most people said couldn't be done this fast. @wildernests — homes launching May."

**Post 9 — Adventure lifestyle integration**
Visual: Bear at/near a Wildernests structure — cooking outside, waking up in nature, morning run.
Caption: "This is what mornings look like when your home is in the right place."

## WEEK 4 — PRE-LAUNCH BUILD
*Objective: Drive signups/enquiries before May launch. Urgency without pressure.*

**Post 10 — Behind the scenes**
Visual: Wildernests team, architecture renders, construction clips.
Caption: "Launch month. Here's what we've been building. @wildernests"

**Post 11 — Direct call to action**
Visual: Clean graphic or Bear to camera.
Script/Caption: "We're opening the first homes in May. If you've been watching — now's the time. Link in bio. @wildernests"

**Post 12 — Personal endorsement (Bear)**
Visual: Bear, direct to camera, genuine and unscripted.
Script: "I've backed a lot of things in my career. The ones I believe in most are the ones that solve a real problem in a genuinely new way. Wildernests does that. I want you to see it."
