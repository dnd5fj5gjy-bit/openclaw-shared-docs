## Three conflicts that need resolving before May 21

### 1. Modern Savage vs Organised Nutrition
These are both organ supplement products. If Bear has equity in Organised and is also the face of Modern Savage, there is a conflict — either in fact or in public perception. Jesse handles Organised directly and has been told not to touch it, which suggests there's sensitivity here. **Before Modern Savage scales its Bear-branded marketing, this needs to be understood.**

### 2. BGSA exclusivity scope
BGSA holds an exclusive license for Bear's survival education brand. "Survival education" is broad. It could technically cover: Adventure Garage (vehicle survival skills), BecomingX (mental resilience content), Running Wild (survival scenarios), and any future survival-focused content deal. The boundary between Bear's general content and BGSA's licensed territory is probably not clearly drawn. **Any new content deal with a survival angle should be checked against the BGSA license first.**

### 3. Natural Studios exit option + EBG relationship
Bear holds 39% of Natural Studios (JV with Delbert + Banijay Group). He has an exit option at end of 2026. If he exercises it, the question is what happens to Running Wild, Adventure Garage, and the EBG consulting agreement — all of which flow through Natural Studios. EBG's $25K/month is tied to shows. If Bear exits Natural Studios, what is EBG's standing? **This needs to be understood before the exit option window arrives in December 2026.**

## Fast-screen questions for new pitches

Before presenting any deal to Bear, run through these:

1. Is it a supplement, nutrition, or wellness brand? → Check against Modern Savage + Organised + Mettle.
2. Is it a home, land, or off-grid living brand? → Check against Wildernests + Water2.
3. Is it a mental health, coaching, or personal development brand? → Check against Mettle + BecomingX.
4. Is it a survival, outdoor, or military fitness brand? → Check against BGSA + BMF.
5. Is it a TV/film/content production deal? → Check against Natural Studios + EBG.
6. Is it an AI representation deal? → Check against Twyn.

If the answer is yes to any of the above, confirm the existing contract's exclusivity clause before proceeding.
