## The Rule Before Anything Else

Bear does not post ads. He posts things he believes in.

Every post below is written from Bear's perspective as a person who takes organ supplements and has a reason for it — not as a brand ambassador. If any post feels like a promotion, don't use it. The test: would Bear post this if Modern Savage didn't exist and he was just sharing what he does?

**Visual direction for all posts:** Real context. Field, training, outdoors, kitchen, early morning. Not a studio. Not a white background. Not a product shot. Bear with the product is fine — but Bear doing something while the product is incidental is better.
