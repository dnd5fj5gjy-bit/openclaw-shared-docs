Key Decisions Now Pricing is Confirmed

  • Launch with adult only or both SKUs? Recommended: adult first. Kids follows in Month 2 once you have conversion data. Simpler ops, lower cash risk.
  • 2,500 or 5,000 units? Start at 2,500 ($61K). The $0.93/unit saving at 5,000 only matters if you sell through — don't over-commit inventory on a new product.
  • Retail price confirmation: $69.99 for adult gives 48.6% contribution margin and $34 max CAC. This is the right price. Don't go lower.
  • Subscription pricing: $59.99/month subscription (14% discount) still gives $23.56 contribution margin at 2,500 MOQ. Worth offering from day one — LTV dramatically improves on subscribers.
  • Production lead time: Ask Andrew how long from order confirmation to delivery. This determines whether July launch is achievable — if lead time is 10+ weeks, order must be placed by mid-May.
