Recommendation

MODERN SAVAGE and MINI SAVAGE (both 600g)

Use Salty's stock 1kg size: 235 x 340 x 110mm
No custom tooling. Melissa can quote immediately. No lead time penalty.

The math: Organ blend powder bulk density is approximately 0.45-0.50 g/mL (similar to greens powders and superfood blends). A 600g fill requires roughly 1,200-1,330 mL. Salty's 1kg stock is designed for up to 2,000 mL at this density - so a 600g fill runs at roughly 60-65% capacity. With nitrogen flush from Lightning Nutra, the headspace is inert and invisible to the consumer.

Why stock is the right call right now:

  • Zero custom tooling cost - custom pouch tooling adds $800-1,500 upcharge and 4-8 weeks lead time
  • Both 600g SKUs share a single pouch - one buy, lower per-unit cost
  • A slightly generous bag reads as premium, not hollow (standard in the supplement industry)
  • Melissa can quote today without further back-and-forth

If the 1kg footprint is too large for your outer box or mailer design, the custom fallback is:

Custom 600g: 205 x 285 x 110mm - a 6% scale-up from the 500g stock, snug fit, clean on shelf

BIG SUMMIT STACK (280g)

Custom: 160 x 225 x 85mm

Salty has no suitable stock size for 280g. The 500g stock (190x265x100mm) would leave the bag ~40% empty - not acceptable. Scaling proportionally down from the 500g stock (scale factor 0.84) gives 160 x 225 x 85mm, which provides approximately 720-800 mL effective capacity - correct fill efficiency at 280g and organ blend density.

WVTR - What to Verify in the PDF

From Melissa's tech sheet, check these three numbers:

  • WVTR (Water Vapour Transmission Rate) - supplement-grade powders need <1.0 g/m²/day. Acceptable for compostable with barrier coating: 0.2-0.5 g/m²/day. If she doesn't quote it, ask explicitly.
  • OTR (Oxygen Transmission Rate) - organ powders oxidise. Look for <50 cc/m²/day.
  • Nitrogen-flush compatibility - confirm the seal integrity holds at Lightning Nutra's filling line pressure.
