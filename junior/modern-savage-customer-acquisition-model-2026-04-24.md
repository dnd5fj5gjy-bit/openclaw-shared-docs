After first customers have had a week to try the product, activate referrals. Offer: "Give a friend a free month, get a free month." CAC drops to $15-25. Viral potential exists in the ancestral health community.

Don't start paid until the organic and referral flywheel is turning. Reason: you want the landing page and email experience to be proven before you pay $100 to send someone to it.
