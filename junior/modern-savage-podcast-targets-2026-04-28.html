The Right Angle

This is not a supplement pitch. No podcast worth having Bear on will frame it that way, and Bear shouldn't either.

The conversation that opens every door on this list: "What happens to human performance when you strip away modernity?" Bear has lived the answer. He's eaten raw organs in the Arctic. He knows what the body does when you feed it what it was designed for. Modern Savage is the civilian version of that knowledge — but the conversation is about ancestral living, not a product.

The product comes up naturally: "What made you put this into a supplement?" One question, one honest answer. That's all it needs.
