### Tuesday April 28 — Air day

**Pre-air (afternoon):** Simple countdown. "Tonight. 9PM. Tiffany Haddish. Fox." Don't over-produce this.

**Post-air (9:30-10:30PM ET):** If there are episode clips available via Fox/Hulu, repost the most shareable moment. Tiffany's reactions are the content — she's a performer and will have generated quotable/shareable moments throughout.

**Key:** The post-air window (first hour after broadcast) is when engagement is highest for live TV. If Calvin can schedule a reactive post to go 30 minutes into the episode, that's the money slot.

## What to Avoid

- Don't use generic "tune in!" language. Bear's audience has seen that a thousand times.
- Don't put Fox/Hulu logos all over it — it reads as an ad, not authentic content.
- Don't caption with episode synopsis. The show sells itself; Bear's voice sells the moment.
- Don't post more than once per day in this window. Quality over volume.
