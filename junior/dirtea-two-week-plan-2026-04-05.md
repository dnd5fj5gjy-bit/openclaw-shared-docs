# DIRTEA Two-Week Plan

**Date:** 2026-04-05  
**Prepared by:** Junior

## Objective
Help DIRTEA do more and do better over the next two weeks by reducing dependence on high-CAC Meta spend, tightening the message, and building a better route into the US.

## What the email trail says
- DIRTEA is a real priority in Jesse's world.
- The brand has already had shareholder / growth / LTV / CRM discussions.
- There has been a previous willingness to sell or reshape the business if it helps.
- The current problem is not awareness alone; it is likely conversion efficiency, message specificity, and US distribution.

## Two-week plan

### Week 1 — Diagnose and sharpen
**Goal:** get very clear on what is broken before trying to scale anything.

1. **Audit the current offer and positioning**
   - What is the hero product?
   - What exact problem does it solve?
   - What is the best one-line promise?
   - What is the weakest part of the current US pitch?

2. **Review Meta CAC by angle**
   - Split ads by message angle, not just by spend.
   - Identify the top 1-2 hooks that convert and the bottom 1-2 that burn cash.
   - Kill the weakest angle fast.

3. **Tighten the US narrative**
   - Move from "functional mushrooms" to one outcome-led story.
   - Pick the primary hero use case: focus, calm energy, recovery, or better mornings.
   - Write a simple founder point of view for the US market.

4. **Map the trust stack**
   - What proof exists already?
   - What testimonials, reviews, ingredients, or experts can be surfaced?
   - What is missing that would help a US buyer trust the brand faster?

### Week 2 — Build new channels and tests
**Goal:** reduce CAC pressure by adding better demand sources and distribution paths.

5. **Launch a creator / authority test**
   - Pick 10 niche creators or practitioners.
   - Prefer people who can talk credibly about focus, stress, productivity, wellness, or performance.
   - Give them a clean angle and one strong product story.

6. **Test a partner-led US wedge**
   - Explore premium retail, affiliates, wellness operators, or founder-to-founder partnerships.
   - Build one distribution route that is not dependent on Meta.

7. **Create one conversion asset**
   - A sharper landing page.
   - A simple quiz.
   - A comparison page.
   - Or a hero guide that explains why DIRTEA exists and why it matters.

8. **Build a daily content rhythm**
   - Short founder POV posts.
   - One proof-based post.
   - One product use-case post.
   - One myth-busting post.
   - Keep it sharp, not fluffy.

## My opinion
DIRTEA probably needs less broad wellness marketing and more specific belief-building.
If the US is struggling, the answer is probably not more generic spend. It is tighter positioning, better proof, and better channels.

## Immediate action list for Jesse
- Pick the one US use case DIRTEA should own.
- Cut one wasting Meta angle.
- Identify 10 credible voices to test.
- Build one partner wedge.
- Rewrite the brand around the outcome, not the ingredient.
