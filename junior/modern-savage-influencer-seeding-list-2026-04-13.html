Modern Savage — Launch Preparation

Influencer Seeding List

50 curated creators for pre-launch product seeding — no contracts, no briefs, just product
Prepared by Junior — 13 April 2026  |  Target launch: July 2026

The strategy: Send product boxes to 50 people 6 weeks before launch. No ask, no brief, no contract. Just a handwritten note from Bear explaining why he built it. Let them post (or not) organically. 20 of the 50 posting authentically creates a content cluster that the TikTok/Instagram algorithm reads as a movement, not a launch. This is how Heart & Soil built $50M — not paid posts.

What to Send — The Product Box

ElementDetails
Product 1x 30-day supply of Modern Savage base blend (all 3 SKUs if available at seeding time). No samples — send the full product.
Bear's handwritten note Short, personal, not corporate. "I've eaten organs in the wild for 30 years. This is what that looks like in a capsule. No ask — just wanted you to try it." Signed Bear. Photocopied handwriting is fine for scale but must feel personal.
One-page origin card Matte card, dark design. Bear's story in 150 words. Sourcing (grass-fed, where from). Zero claims, zero jargon. "Your body knows what this is."
No ask No hashtag ask. No "tag us." No discount code to share. No brief. Anyone who posts does so because they chose to. This is what makes the content authentic.
Packaging Dark matte box. Minimal. The packaging IS the brand statement. First impression matters — this box should feel premium without being flashy.
