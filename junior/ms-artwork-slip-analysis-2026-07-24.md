## 2. What the slip costs

The published chain was: artwork to Belmark by Fri 24 Jul, pouches 8 Sep, launch 12 Sep. Every day past today moves 8 Sep day for day.

Two things make that worse than a simple one-day-per-day slide:

**The weekend is free but it is not free.** Missing Friday does not cost one day, it costs three, because nothing at Belmark or ACF moves until Monday 27 Jul. Even a perfect Monday puts us three calendar days down with no work done.

**The quote expires on 30 July.** The 30 June quote is valid to 30 Jul. If artwork lands in August the pricing is not automatically ours any more, and re-quoting is both a delay and a commercial exposure. We asked Sarah to confirm she would hold pricing and **she has never confirmed it**. That confirmation is now the cheapest, most valuable thing on the list, and it is a question to Belmark, who answer, not to ACF, who do not.

Also live, from the 30 June quote: prepress is $150 per SKU and is charged again on any later artwork change, so a rushed wrong file costs $450 to correct. Freight is excluded. Tolerance is +15%/-10%.

**The 15-day Belmark lead time is still an assumption.** Nobody at Belmark has confirmed it, and the entire 8 Sep date rests on it. If it is really 20 days, 12 Sep was already gone before today. This is the single unverified number holding up the whole plan.
