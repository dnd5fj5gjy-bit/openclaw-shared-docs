# Amazon / FBA / AI Meeting Booklet
**Jim Reed meeting**  
**Date:** 2026-03-26  
**Prepared by:** Junior

## The punchline
- **Newgate** is the best Amazon growth candidate right now.
- **Funkstar** is a strong second: design-led, giftable, seasonal, easy to automate.
- **London Mole** can work, but should be run selectively around best-selling reader SKUs, not a broad catalogue push.
- The biggest opportunity is not “more ad management” but **automation**: stock-aware bidding, listing-to-campaign creation, coupon triggers, review sentiment loops, and FBA inventory controls.
- Amazon should be the **volume capture channel**; DTC should be the **brand + margin + CRM layer**.

## What to say in the room
### 1) Where the money is
- Sponsored Products first.
- Hero ASIN focus, not bloated catalogue spend.
- Tight Brand Store / Sponsored Brands on brand terms.
- Add Display/DSP only after the basics are disciplined.

### 2) What AI can do better than humans
- Auto-build campaigns from listings.
- Adjust bids and budgets daily against ACOS, CVR, CPC, and stock cover.
- Spot coupon windows and competitor moves.
- Mine reviews for winning copy, objections, and negative keywords.
- Forecast FBA replenishment and flag aged inventory before fees bite.

### 3) Where margin leaks today
- Wasted ad spend on weak keywords.
- Stockouts on winners.
- Aged inventory and overstocked tail SKUs.
- Promo timing done too late.
- Too many low-priority ASINs competing for spend.

## 5 recommended next actions
1. **Pick Newgate as pilot brand** and isolate top 10-20 hero ASINs.
2. **Export 12 months of Amazon data**: sales, ads, inventory age, fees, returns, sessions, conversion.
3. **Give Felix API access**: SP-API + Amazon Ads API + current ASIN master.
4. **Build the first automation layer**: stock-aware Sponsored Products bidding + alerting + campaign generator.
5. **Roll to Funkstar next**, then decide whether London Mole deserves selective Amazon scale or a DTC-first posture.

## Felix build ask
- Warehouse + connectors first
- Rules engine second
- Dashboard and approvals third
- Start lean, prove lift, then expand

## Decision recommendation
**Do not try to automate everything at once.** Start with Newgate, prove better spend efficiency and stock health in 30-45 days, then scale the operating system across the other brands.
