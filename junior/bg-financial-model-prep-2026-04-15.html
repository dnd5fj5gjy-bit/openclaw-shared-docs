Sanity Check

Assumptions Zain will need confirmed

These are the inputs where Zain is most likely to have estimated or assumed. Jesse and CJ should come ready to correct or confirm each one.

Running Wild / Netflix annual fee

Bear's media production deals are the largest single income stream. Zain will have estimated an annual figure for Running Wild S9+10, National Geographic, Netflix etc. What is the actual contracted fee per season? Is it per episode, per season, or a lump sum? This is likely the biggest number in the model.

Total brand licensing income

Luminox is confirmed ($100K/yr from 2027) but Bear licenses his brand across many products. What is the total annual licensing royalty figure? Zain will have a number — Jesse needs to know whether it's in the right ballpark.

Luminox 2026 royalty

The 2027 figure is confirmed at $100K/yr. But what is the 2026 actual royalty? "On actual royalties" — has Bear received or been promised any royalty payment for 2026, or is the contract effectively silent on this year?

Modern Savage year 1 revenue projection

Zain may have modelled this as $0 or used a placeholder. If he's put a number in for 2026, it needs to be flagged as speculative — there's no confirmed pre-sales or distribution deal yet. July launch with $79/bag DTC, target 1,000 customers in launch window = ~$79K gross in month 1 as a rough ceiling.

Ted's Health — loss or write-off?

The model needs to decide whether Ted's Health is modelled as a loss-making ongoing cost, or whether there's a scenario where the white-label pivot changes the economics. At £23K/mo net loss, this is a £276K/yr drag. Does the model account for a breakeven timeline or a potential exit?

Wildernests equity value

0.5% equity agreed but not yet documented. The OSC agreement hasn't been drafted. For the financial model, this is an asset of uncertain value — worth flagging that the equity docs need executing before it can be included as a confirmed asset.

Speaking fees — annual total

Bear commands significant corporate speaking fees. Is there a consistent annual figure Zain should use? This varies year to year but is typically a material income line for public figures of Bear's profile.

Book royalties — ongoing vs one-time

Bear's published books generate ongoing royalties via Penguin and others. Zain needs the actual annual royalty figure, not a guess. Is there an advance against royalties still being recouped, or are these clean royalty payments?
