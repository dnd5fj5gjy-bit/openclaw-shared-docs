# Draft 1 — /shipping

## Shipping Policy

This policy explains when Modern Savage products ship, where we ship to, what it costs, and what to expect once your order is placed. It applies to all orders placed through modernsavage.co.

### Where we ship

- **United Kingdom** — full service. Mainland and Northern Ireland.
- **United States** — full service. All 50 states plus DC.
- **Rest of world** — not currently available. We're working on it. Join the waitlist at modernsavage.co for country expansion announcements.

### When orders ship

Orders placed by 12:00 noon UK time / 7:00am ET on a working day (Monday-Friday, excluding UK and US bank holidays) are dispatched the same working day from our UK fulfilment partner. Orders placed after the cut-off ship the next working day.

**Founding member orders placed during the launch window (July 1-7, 2026):** if you ordered as one of our founding 170, your bottle ships [SHIP DATE — to be filled when confirmed]. You will receive a tracking email when your order leaves the warehouse.

### Delivery times

| Destination | Service | Estimate |
|---|---|---|
| UK mainland | Standard tracked | 2-3 working days |
| UK mainland | Next-day tracked | 1 working day if ordered by noon |
| Northern Ireland | Standard tracked | 3-5 working days |
| US (lower 48) | Standard tracked | 5-7 working days |
| US (Alaska, Hawaii) | Standard tracked | 7-10 working days |

These are estimates from our courier partners, not guarantees. Bank holidays, weather and customs can extend them. We'll always email you the tracking number so you can see where your order is.

### Shipping costs

- **UK orders £40 or above** — free standard shipping.
- **UK orders below £40** — £4.95 standard shipping.
- **UK next-day** — £6.95 (subject to postcode).
- **US orders $50 or above** — free standard shipping.
- **US orders below $50** — $7.95 standard shipping.
- **Subscription orders** — free standard shipping in both UK and US, on every order, regardless of value.

### Customs and duties

UK and US customers do not pay customs or duties. We fulfil from a UK warehouse (UK orders) and a US warehouse (US orders) so your order is a domestic shipment.

### Tracking

Every order is tracked. You'll receive a confirmation email when you order, a dispatch email with a tracking number when the order leaves the warehouse, and a delivery confirmation when it arrives.

### Lost, late or damaged orders

If your order has not arrived within the estimated delivery window plus three working days, email support@modernsavage.co with your order number. We'll either resend or refund.

If your order arrives damaged, take a photo of the packaging and the product before opening anything else, and email it to support@modernsavage.co within 7 days of delivery. We'll resend at no cost or refund in full.

### Address changes

You can change your delivery address up until the order is dispatched. Email support@modernsavage.co with your order number and the new address. Once an order is in the courier's hands we cannot change the destination.

### Returns

See our [Refund Policy](/refunds) for the full process. Short version: 14 days to change your mind, 30 days if the product is faulty, full refund either way.

### Questions

support@modernsavage.co — we aim to reply within one working day.

# Draft 2 — /refunds

## Refund Policy

This policy explains your rights to cancel, return and refund any Modern Savage order. It is written to comply with the UK Consumer Contracts (Information, Cancellation and Additional Charges) Regulations 2013, the Consumer Rights Act 2015, and equivalent US consumer protection law. Whichever set of rules gives you the better outcome is the one we apply.

### Your right to cancel — 14 day cooling-off period

Under UK law you have 14 calendar days from the day you receive your order to cancel for any reason, no questions asked.

To cancel, email support@modernsavage.co with your order number and the words "I want to cancel my order." You don't have to give a reason.

Once we confirm the cancellation, return the unopened product to:
> Modern Savage Returns
> [Ships A Lot UK fulfilment address — to be added before launch]

You pay the cost of return postage unless the product is faulty. We recommend a tracked service — until the product reaches us it remains your responsibility.

We refund the full purchase price (including original outbound shipping if you returned the entire order) within 14 days of receiving the returned product, to the original payment method.

### Opened bottles

You can open and try the product. If you change your mind after opening, you can still return within 14 days. We may deduct an amount from the refund to reflect the reduced value of the product (typically 25-50% depending on how much has been used). This is in line with the Consumer Contracts Regulations.

### Faulty, damaged or wrong products

If your product is faulty, damaged in transit or different from what you ordered, you have 30 days to reject it and get a full refund — no deductions, and we pay the return postage.

To report a fault: email support@modernsavage.co with your order number and a photo or short description. We'll send a pre-paid return label. Once we receive the product we refund within 14 days.

After 30 days you may still have rights under the Consumer Rights Act 2015 (one repair or replacement, then full refund if the issue persists). Email us — we'll work it out with you.

### Pre-orders

If you ordered during a pre-order window (your order confirmation will say so), you can cancel any time before the order ships and get a full refund within 14 days of cancellation. The 14-day cooling-off period for opened-product changes-of-mind starts the day you receive the product, not the day you ordered.

### Subscriptions

You can cancel a subscription at any time from your account page or by emailing support@modernsavage.co. There is no minimum term and no cancellation fee. If you cancel before the next billing date, no further charge is taken. If you cancel within 14 days of receiving your most recent shipment, see "Your right to cancel" above for refund treatment.

### Refund timing and method

Refunds are processed back to the original payment method. Once we trigger the refund, your bank typically shows the funds within 3-10 working days depending on the card issuer.

### What we do not refund

- Gift cards and store credit.
- Products returned more than 14 days after delivery, unless faulty.
- Products returned damaged, with seal broken in a way that makes resale impossible, beyond the cooling-off "change-your-mind" allowance.
- Original outbound shipping cost if only part of the order is returned.

### US customers

US customers have the same 14-day change-of-mind window. Faulty product returns follow each state's implied warranty rules. California Civil Code §1791 and equivalent state statutes apply where they exceed our policy. Contact support@modernsavage.co for US returns — we'll send a pre-paid USPS or UPS label.

### Founding members

If you ordered as one of the original 170 founding members during the July 1 launch and your product has not yet shipped, you can cancel and get a full refund any time before dispatch. Email support@modernsavage.co with your order number and "Cancel founding order."

### Disputes

We try to resolve every issue before it gets to a dispute. If you're unhappy with how we've handled a return, email Raemy Singh, CFO, at raemy@beargryllsventures.com directly.

UK customers can also escalate to:
- Citizens Advice consumerline 0808 223 1133
- Trading Standards via your local council.

US customers can escalate to:
- The Better Business Bureau (bbb.org)
- Your state Attorney General's consumer protection division.

We aim never to need either of these.
