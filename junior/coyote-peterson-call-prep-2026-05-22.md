## Key Questions to Ask

These are genuine questions — not rhetorical. You want the answers.

1. **"Where do you want to take Brave Wilderness over the next few years — is it more content, or are you trying to build something beyond the channel?"**
   This tells you whether he's building a company or a personal brand. Big difference for any collab structure.

2. **"Have you ever done wildlife content in genuinely remote environments — I mean really off-grid, not documentary-remote?"**
   This tests whether Coyote can operate in Bear-territory. Some creators who do wildlife can't handle true expedition conditions. If he can, that's interesting. If he's been waiting for an excuse to try, even more interesting.

3. **"What's the gap you keep seeing in the outdoor/adventure content space that nobody's filling?"**
   This is the most important question on the call. His answer tells you what he actually wants to build. If he says "depth" or "real stakes" or "prestige" — you're aligned. If he says "more animals" — you're probably not building the same thing.

4. **"Do you have kids? Does the family content angle matter to you personally, or is it purely audience strategy?"**
   This one is both personal (builds relationship) and strategic (Modern Savage crossover). Don't force it — wait for the right moment.
