The UK VAT question

UK dietary supplements (HS 2106.90) typically carry 0% import duty. The real cost is UK import VAT at 20%.

On a ~$120K shipment (product value) + $4.3K freight = $124.3K CIF:

Import VAT = ~$24,860 USD

If Bear Witness Ltd is UK VAT-registered: VAT is fully recoverable on the next VAT return. Net cost to you: ~$4.3K freight + minimal duty = ~$4,500 total.
