# Modern Savage — Customer Support Template Pack
## First 30 Days Post-Launch

**Built:** May 2, 2026 (Pulse 61 — overnight)
**For:** Calvin / Raemy / whoever staffs support@modernsavage.co
**Why:** The 90-day economics model identified churn as the highest-leverage launch lever. Slow or inconsistent support = bad reviews = NPS drop = churn spike. Calvin's SOW does not include support handling. No templates currently exist. Without these, the first 30 days of support is improvised.

**How to use:** Drop these into Helpscout, Front, Gorgias, or whatever support inbox tool gets selected. Names in [BRACKETS] are merge fields. Tone matches the Modern Savage brand voice (direct, grown-up, no hedging, no exclamation marks).

| Channel | Target response time | First-pass resolution target |
|---|---|---|
| support@modernsavage.co | 1 working day | 80% |

| Refund processed (T6, T7) | Within 3 working days of receipt of returned product | 100% |
| Tracked outage / system issue | 4 hours, status page update | n/a |
