UK Customs - What You Need

Andrew Kouvel flagged that UK customs duties and VAT are NOT included in the NTG quote. You need a UK customs broker to handle entry before stock can move to the UK 3PL.

What to budget

Import duty
Dietary supplements (HS 2106.90): 0-5.5% depending on exact classification. Andrew or a customs broker can confirm. At $82K goods value: $0-4,500.
UK VAT
20% standard rate on supplements in powder form. On $82K goods: ~$16,400. You reclaim this via UK VAT registration (Sara Perry at David Owen handles this).
Broker fee
~£500-1,500 one-time for customs entry + HMRC classification. One-time cost.
VAT is recoverable once Modern Savage is VAT-registered in the UK. Sara Perry (David Owen Accountancy) is handling UK VAT registration - check status. If not yet registered, UK customs VAT becomes an upfront cash cost you recover later.
