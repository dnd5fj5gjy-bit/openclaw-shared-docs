## 3. Why Manufacture in the UK (the case, stated once)

1. **Landed cost:** the container route was $2,496-$4,296 + duty + VAT + a lapsed HTS-code question + 19 days on the water. UK manufacturing removes all of it.
2. **Speed:** UK reorders in up to 8 weeks domestically vs US production + ocean freight + customs.
3. **Compliance clean-break:** UK-made product gets UK labelling/FBO/HACCP natively rather than retrofitting US product.
4. **Redundancy:** two qualified manufacturers holding our spec ends single-vendor dependence on ACF - this is also the honest fix to the spec-sheet hostage dynamic.
5. **Market logic:** Bear's audience is UK-heavy; £65/£55 UK pricing supports UK COGS comfortably.

**Alignment note:** Bear's 1 Jul steer was "keep him [Drew] focussed on US for now" - that was about not distracting ACF, not against a UK track. The UK build runs on our side (specs, Protein Partners, FBO) and touches Drew only for the spec handover. Worth a one-line confirmation with Bear so everyone's rowing the same way.
