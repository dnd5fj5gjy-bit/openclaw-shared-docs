### TRACK 3 — LEGAL ENTITY + STRIPE (Blocker)

Bear asked April 17: "Do we need modern savage to be US company?" CJ and Raemy were still discussing as of April 17. CJ proposed "wholly owned BW subsidiary."

**The Stripe problem:** Bear Witness Ltd (Isle of Man) cannot open a Stripe account. IOM is explicitly excluded from Stripe's supported countries. The options:
- **Option A:** New UK Ltd (cheap, fast — same-day Companies House). Name: Modern Savage Ltd or similar.
- **Option B:** US LLC or C-Corp (slower, more expensive, more appropriate if US-primary business).
- **Option C:** Bear Witness Ltd UK subsidiary (CJ's proposal).

Bear's instinct was right to ask about US entity. Modern Savage is a US product, US customers, US 3PL (Ships A Lot), US manufacturer. A US entity makes financial and operational sense long-term. But incorporating a US entity takes 2-4 weeks and then 2-3 more weeks for a US bank account.

**Timeline pressure:** If a US entity is needed and not started by May 5, Stripe will not be live by July 1.

**Raemy's action:** Confirm entity decision and begin filing. This should have happened the week of April 21.

**Status: AMBER — if entity has been decided and UK Ltd filed, this is recoverable. If still undecided, urgently escalate.**
