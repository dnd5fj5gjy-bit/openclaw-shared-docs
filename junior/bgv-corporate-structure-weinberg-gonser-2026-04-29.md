## The Double Taxation / Entity Question

**The issue Weinberg Gonser will flag:** Bear Witness Limited is incorporated in the Isle of Man, not the UK or US. The IoM is a Crown Dependency — it has its own tax treaties and is NOT covered by the US-UK tax treaty for most purposes.

**Relevant implications for the trademark filing:**
- A trademark can be filed in the name of Bear Witness Ltd (IoM) — but enforcement of a US trademark held by an IoM entity has been done successfully before.
- Alternatively, the trademark could be filed in the name of a UK company (if Modern Savage Ltd UK is incorporated) or a US LLC.
- The double taxation concern most likely relates to: royalty income from the US (if the brand licenses IP), or direct US sales revenue flowing back to an IoM entity.

**What Weinberg Gonser needs to advise on:**
1. Which entity should be the USPTO trademark applicant — Bear Witness Ltd (IoM), Modern Savage Ltd UK [CONFIRM status], or a new US LLC?
2. If a US entity is recommended, does Jesse want to incorporate one (e.g., Modern Savage Inc, Delaware)?
