Worked example · ED medication

Sold straight off the quiz — safely

The ED pathway already captures everything a prescriber needs, so the consultation becomes a confirmation, not a workup.

What the quiz screens
Erection severity, what’s been tried, allergies to sildenafil / tadalafil / vardenafil / avanafil, and the full interaction screen — nitrates & nicorandil, GTN, alpha-blockers, cimetidine, macrolides, azoles, HIV meds, blood-pressure & heart-rhythm meds.
What the doctor gets
A pre-brief flagging the risks and eligibility before they open the consult. If a red flag is present (e.g. nitrates), it’s surfaced — not buried in a form. The clinician reviews, not re-interviews.
What the clinic sells
A safe, clinician-approved ED prescription dispatched to the patient — the classic quiz-to-medication funnel, but with the clinical safety and audit trail built in and CQC-defensible.
Shorter
Consults start from a structured brief, not a blank page — less clinician time per patient.
Safer
Contraindications & red flags screened before prescribing — the compliance work is done up front.
Higher
Every assessment ends in a named, bookable next step — conversion, not a PDF.
Recurring
Re-assessment and monitoring drive repeat scripts and re-orders over time.

Screening guidance, not a diagnosis. Prescribing decisions stay with the clinician; the platform does the intake, the safety screen and the audit trail around them.
