# HealthPilot white-label — premium offering brief (8 Jul 2026)
Research (3 streams) + council synthesis + prioritised backlog. Internal.

## Council verdict (Killer / Stranger / Operator / CFO / Chair)
- **The real product isn't the quiz** — it's the closed loop: branded lead-gen → clinician-safe triage/escalation → revenue routing → retention. Lead the story with that; quiz aesthetics are table stakes.
- **Biggest cheapener is dev chrome + generic brand**, not the design. Kill emulator banners/cookie modals/placeholder logos on anything a buyer sees. (Done for the video.)
- **Restraint sells premium** — "show every feature" must stay a narrative, not a feature-dump.
- **Don't over-build for a video** — the concierge preset already reads premium; polish + wearables + clean chrome beats a risky ground-up rebuild.

## What the video demonstrates (latest integrated build)
Branded front door → considered quiz → wearables Recovery & Readiness (preview) → results nudge/upsell ("recommended for you, entirely up to you") → retention/history → clinic analytics → clinical-safety escalations → self-serve setup (toggles/language/embed) → live re-brand (customiseable) → owner console (billing/keys/quiz versioning).

## Premium quiz — top levers (research 1)
Warm ivory ground; editorial serif questions set light; one question per screen; expo-out cross-fade transitions; warm short copy + reason-why; human-worded options (not 1-5); live "what we're seeing" insight rail (highest premium signal); one restrained accent; hairline borders not shadow cards; contextual privacy cues; staged narrative results reveal. Ship 3 curated brand presets (Ivory/Forest, Charcoal/Amber, Warm/Clay) not a free colour picker.

## Upsell — best practice the build already follows (research 2)
Give the result free, gate the ACTION; one personalised clinician-voice CTA; "recommended for you" branched off the answer band and named back; restraint over urgency (NO countdown/scarcity — FTC risk); dual-range (standard + "optimal") flags; bundle test+consult; re-test as the retention loop; two-sided referral at the good-result moment. White-label deal-makers to add: custom domain, full removal of "powered by", authenticated branded email domain, branded PDFs, SSO in base, per-tenant pricing/modules, DPA.

## Wearables — recommended path (research 3)
Build the MVP on **Terra** (tryterra.co): hosted connect widget for Oura/Whoop/Garmin/Fitbit/Apple → pull 30d HRV, resting HR, sleep, VO2max → branded "Recovery & Readiness" panel + a flags/trends block in the clinician brief, behind explicit UK-GDPR consent. ~2-3 weeks to demo, 4-6 weeks to production. $399/mo (~500 users). Keep Vital as the US wearable+labs alternative. The video's wearables scene is a labelled preview of exactly this.

## Prioritised backlog to reach "undeniably premium"
1. Real premium logo for every demo/clinic brand (kills the template look).
2. Wearables MVP with Terra (real connect + panel + clinician-brief block).
3. Quiz polish: insight rail, serif at proper weight, expo-out transitions, warm option copy.
4. White-label depth: custom domain + authenticated branded email + zero vendor footprint (the Healthie-Enterprise features, self-serve).
5. Results reveal as a staged narrative report with one CTA + dual-range flags.
6. 3 curated brand presets so every clinic instance stays premium.
