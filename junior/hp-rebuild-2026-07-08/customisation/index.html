Locked for clinical safety (CQC)

System-managed, versioned and audited. Hidden from clinic admins — no clinic can weaken them.

✓
Red-flag detection & urgent escalation
Dangerous answers always raise a flag and page a human within minutes — non-negotiable.
✓
Clinician review of results
Abnormal results are held for a clinician before the patient acts on them.
✓
"Screening, not a diagnosis" framing
Medical disclaimers and safe wording are enforced on every screen and in the report.
✓
Consent & data protection
Explicit UK-GDPR consent, special-category handling, and one-tap revoke are mandatory.
✓
Full audit trail
Every result, flag, escalation and acknowledgement is logged for a CQC inspection.
✓
Safe recommendation guardrails
Clinics edit copy, but can't recommend beyond clinically-approved pathways.

Result: a clinic can make the platform completely their own, and still can't accidentally build something unsafe.

The principle: customisation is data, not code — one platform, per-clinic config. Everything commercial and cosmetic is a clinic's to set. Everything that protects a patient is the platform's to guarantee. That split is exactly what makes it both sellable and CQC-defensible.
