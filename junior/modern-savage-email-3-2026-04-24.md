# Modern Savage — Email 3: The Tribe Email
**Prepared:** April 24, 2026
**Send timing:** 4-5 days after Email 2 (once reply-"yes" cohort has had time to digest the science)
**Goal:** Social proof. Tribe formation. FOMO signal. Make the waiting feel like belonging, not just waiting.

---

## What Email 3 Must Do

Email 1 built identity. Email 2 built credibility. Now the reader has been through two emails without being asked to buy anything. They respect that. They're curious.

Email 3 pivots from "why this exists" to "who else is already here." The psychology: humans are tribal. We don't just want to know a product works — we want to know people like us are already involved. The founding member cohort is the product at this stage. Show them who's waiting.

This email is not a testimonial email. It's a "look what's forming" email. The reader should feel: *I want to be part of this before it closes.*

**Critical constraint:** At the time of writing, the product hasn't launched. We don't have real testimonials. The quotes below are templates — they should be replaced with real replies from the Email 1 "yes" cohort before sending. Calvin should collect 5-10 genuine responses and curate the best three.

If real quotes aren't available in time: use the template structure with lightly paraphrased versions of the most common response types the brand has received. Never fabricate direct attribution.
