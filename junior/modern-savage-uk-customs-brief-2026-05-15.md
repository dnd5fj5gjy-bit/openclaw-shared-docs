## The POAO Problem

The organ blend — specifically the freeze-dried bovine liver, heart, spleen, kidney, and lung — makes this a Product of Animal Origin. That triggers a different set of UK border controls than a standard supplement.

**What POAO means in practice:**

1. **Pre-notification via IPAFFS:** The importer (or their customs broker) must pre-notify APHA (Animal and Plant Health Agency) via the Import of Products, Animals, Food and Feed System (IPAFFS) at least **24-48 hours before the vessel arrives at Felixstowe**. No notification = shipment held indefinitely.

2. **Export Health Certificate from USDA-FSIS:** Lightning Nutra must obtain a USDA-FSIS Export Health Certificate (EHC) for food of animal origin destined for Great Britain. This is issued by a USDA-accredited veterinarian and confirms the product meets UK import requirements. Lightning Nutra (Andrew/John) should know if they've done this before — if not, it needs to be requested from USDA now.

3. **GB Approved US Establishments List:** The manufacturing facility — Lightning Nutra, Pompano Beach, FL — must be listed on the UK's approved list of US food establishments authorised to export food of animal origin to Great Britain. This list is maintained by APHA. **If they're not on it, the shipment cannot legally enter the UK.** Verifying this is the single most important call of the next 48 hours. Ask the customs broker to check against the FSA/APHA database.

4. **Border Control Post (BCP) inspection at Felixstowe:** All POAO consignments are physically inspected at the BCP. Felixstowe is an authorised BCP for POAO — correct routing. Inspection fees typically run £100-300 per consignment. Expect 1-3 day hold at the BCP while paperwork is verified.

## LCL vs FCL — Consider This Before Confirming the Quote

NTG quoted for a **20-foot full container load (FCL)**. For 5,000 units of supplement powder, estimated cargo weight is 3-4 metric tons — well under FCL capacity (20-25 tons). You are paying for empty space.

**LCL (Less than Container Load)** may cost 30-50% less for this volume. Ask George Baker or KWL to quote LCL from Pompano Beach or Miami to Felixstowe before confirming the NTG FCL rate. The trade-off is slightly less scheduling flexibility — but for a first shipment, the cost saving is material.
