Situation

Where the Book Stands

GSET launched June 2025 with a No.1 Sunday Times debut. 4.7 stars with nearly 2,000 reviews. Bestseller status in Christian Church History on Amazon. By all measures, a strong Year 1 in the UK. The challenge Andy/Hodder brought Jesse in to solve: Year 2 international sales.

Year 2 is always harder for a faith book. The initial wave of core believers bought early. The growth now comes from two directions: reaching faith communities in new markets (especially the US) and breaking through to the cultural middle — people who don't identify as religious but respect Bear, are curious about Jesus, or will be pulled in by the surrounding cultural moment.

The timing in summer/autumn 2026 is genuinely exceptional. Three major cultural events align with GSET's content. This brief is built around exploiting all three.

UK

Home Market — Defend

Remain visible. Hay Festival + JCS give two major PR windows this summer. Don't let the book go quiet.

US

Primary Growth Market

Largest English-language Christian book market in the world. Bear has 100M+ monthly impressions via Fox/Running Wild. GSET has not broken through here yet. This is the prize.

AU+ZA

Secondary Anglophone Markets

Strong faith communities, English-language, Bear highly recognised. Lighter lift than the US but meaningful volume.

2026 / 2027 Opportunity

Three Cultural Hooks

These are not manufactured PR pegs. They are live, high-profile cultural events that put the story of Jesus at the centre of public conversation. GSET is the perfect companion book for all three.

Hook 1 — Summer 2026, Live Now

Jesus Christ Superstar at the London Palladium

The award-winning Regent's Park Open Air Theatre production is transferring to the London Palladium for summer 2026. Cast: Sam Ryder as Jesus (Eurovision finalist, massive following), Tyrone Huntley as Judas, Boy George / Richard Armitage / Jesse Tyler Ferguson rotating as King Herod. This is a mainstream cultural event, not a niche faith production. It will generate enormous press coverage and put the story of Jesus in the secular spotlight all summer.

GSET angle: "Before you see it at the Palladium, read the account that started 2,000 years ago. Bear Grylls' eyewitness retelling of the same story — told through the eyes of someone who was there." Link directly to the production's promotional window. Approach the Palladium/LW Theatres for co-promotion: GSET in programme inserts, links in email newsletters, content crossover. This is not a paid media play — it's earned through the shared subject matter.
Hook 2 — Good Friday 26 March 2027 (pre-marketing starts now)

Mel Gibson's "The Resurrection of the Christ"

The sequel to The Passion of the Christ (2004 box office: $612M), distributed by Lionsgate. Two-part release: Part 1 on Good Friday March 26, 2027, Part 2 on Ascension Day May 6, 2027. Now filming in Italy. Jim Caviezel not returning; Finnish actor Jaakko Ohtonen as Jesus. The build-up marketing for this film — set for global release — will be the largest mainstream cultural focus on Jesus since 2004. The pre-release window starts this autumn.

GSET angle: Position GSET as "the book to read before the film." By the time the film releases, GSET should be embedded in every Christian retail, streaming platform, and faith community as the companion read. Work with Andy/Hodder NOW to secure a tie-in edition (potentially with a "before the film" cover wrap). Approach Lionsgate's international marketing teams for potential co-promotion. This is an 18-month flywheel that Jesse should be seeding now.
Hook 3 — Late May / June 2026

Hay Festival + Shara's Book Launch

Bear and Shara are both at Hay-on-Wye this season. Shara just launched "Never Stop Holding Hands." This is a double Grylls presence at the UK's leading literary festival — the natural media event to remind literary audiences that GSET exists and is still generating conversation. Andrew Smiles's family connection (introduced via Bertie Portal) is attending, with Lizzie Birkelund (Bear's relative, author) also at Hay.

GSET angle: Bear + Shara double event at Hay is a PR gift. Work with Hodder's PR team to get GSET prominently stocked at the Hay bookshop and in any Hay press coverage of the Grylls family. Use Bear's Hay appearance to do a brief faith/family/Jesus content moment for social — this creates immediate Hay-week GSET visibility. This is a short window but a free one.
