## What Henri-Lloyd Actually Is

Premium British sailing and outdoor brand. Founded 1963. They invented the oilskin sailing jacket. They outfit offshore racing sailors and adventurous amateurs alike. Their aesthetic: serious kit for people who actually go. Not fashion, not performance cosplay — functional gear with decades of provenance.

Jesse's positioning within that brand: the adventure bloodline. Bear's son. Someone who grew up on boats and expeditions. Not a celebrity who happens to hold a sail — a person who actually belongs on the water.

This is a rare fit. Most ambassador deals are a stretch. This one is not.

## The Sailing Trip as Content Calendar

### Before Departure (May 5-6)
**Purpose:** Announcement + kit reveal

Post idea 1 — gear check:
> "Heading out to sea for two weeks. The kit has to be right. @henrilloyd has been with sailors like this since 1963. That history isn't nothing when you're offshore."

Photo: flat lay or wearable — Ocean Pro jacket, Elite trousers, sailing gloves, chart on a table. Crisp, functional aesthetic.

Post idea 2 — the brief (optional story):
Simple: Henri-Lloyd kit against a chart or compass. No caption needed beyond tag + sailing emoji. Stories format, no archive pressure.

---

### At Sea (May 7-21)
**No pressure to post in real time** — Jesse should be sailing, not managing content. But if he's near the coast or has signal, one or two shots from on the water would be exceptional.

**Shot list to have in mind:**
- Dawn watch — hood up, jacket visible, horizon in background
- Helm shot from behind — the silhouette with HL kit is clean
- Hands on the wheel or rope work — detail shot, logo not required
- Crew moment — candid, authentic, not posed

Even one good shot from offshore is worth three posed shots from a dock.

---

### Return (May 21-22)
**Purpose:** Recap content. The narrative writes itself.

Post idea — the return:
> "Two weeks. Offshore. Most of it cold and most of it worth it. Kit held. Body held. That's the report."

Photo: worn kit after a long passage. Salt-stained jacket or well-used gear. Authenticity over polish.

Alternative if minimal photography:
> Short video (30-60 sec) — Jesse speaking direct to camera, on the boat or just off. No script. What the trip was. How the kit performed. Henri-Lloyd doesn't need a polished ad — they need someone who means it.
