Modern Savage — Email 4: The Launch Countdown

Prepared: April 28, 2026 Author: Junior Sequence position: Email 4 of 5 Send timing: 7 days before launch (target: June 24) Goal: Convert warm subscribers into paying founding members before the window closes

Part 1 — What Email 4 Must Do

By the time Email 4 arrives, the subscriber has read:

  • Email 1: Bear's origin story — why he built this
  • Email 2: The ingredient science — what's actually in it
  • Email 3: The daily ritual — how Bear takes it, kids' blend, family angle

They believe in the product. The only remaining friction is urgency and clarity on what they get by acting now vs. waiting.

Email 4 has one job: make the case that now costs less than later, and give them a clear path to act.

What's at stake: This is the last email before launch. Anyone who hasn't bought after this email will face full public pricing on July 1. The founding member price is real, the window is real, and the copy must treat it as real.

What NOT to do: Fake scarcity. "Only 50 spots left!" when there are 170 people on the list and we don't know conversion rate. Bear's list is built on authenticity — the credibility of the founding offer is more powerful than manufactured panic.

The psychological mechanism: Loss aversion activated at the point where the decision has already been emotionally made. The subscriber has opened three emails. They're interested. Email 4 doesn't need to sell the product — it needs to close the already-warm buyer.

Seven days from now, Modern Savage opens to the public.

You can pause or cancel any time. This isn't a trap. The whole point of founding membership is to recognise the people who took a chance on something before it was proven. Trapping people in subscriptions is not how I want to build this.

If you've been reading these emails and thinking "I'll wait and see what the reviews say" — I understand. That's sensible. But I'd rather be honest with you: the founding price is the best you'll get. The product isn't changing. The sourcing isn't changing. What changes next week is who it's available to and what they pay.

If you're ready, the link to sign up is below. Takes about two minutes.
