Commercials

Three deal structures

All three respect Mama Bucci's preference to avoid heavy cash outflow at this growth stage. Recommended option in dark green.

Recommended · Option A

Performance-aligned

Cash $4,000 / month hard costs (production, ad creative, software, ad management)

Equity Tiered, tied to D2C revenue milestones:
  • 2.5% at $1M D2C ARR
  • 2.5% at $5M D2C ARR
  • 2.5% at $10M D2C ARR
Term 24-month commitment. Start: immediately, October launch target.

Low cash burn. Both parties aligned on the same growth curve. BGV only wins big when Mama Bucci wins big. Mirrors the structure already working on Modern Savage.

Option B

Hybrid retainer + bonus

Cash $7,500 / month full service

Performance bonus 5% of net D2C revenue above $1M ARR threshold, capped at $250K / year

Term 12-month rolling

Predictable for both sides. Lower equity dilution. Performance upside still present.

Option C

Full retainer (no equity)

Cash $15,000 / month all-in (tech, content, ads, account management)

Term 12-month rolling

Pure service. Comparable to top D2C agency pricing, with the Bear distribution advantage included rather than paid for separately.

The Bear distribution multiplier A standard D2C agency charges $15–25K/month and delivers ads and content. They cannot deliver audience. One Bear post-launch reel for Mama Bucci can do what a $50K influencer campaign does, on day one, with absolute brand fit. That is the multiplier no other bidder can match.
