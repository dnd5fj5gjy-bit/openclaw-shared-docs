What the UK market needs

Simultaneous US + UK launch is ambitious but the right call — Bear's audience is global and splitting the launch would lose momentum. Key operational items to confirm before go-live.

ItemStatusAction Required
UK pricing confirmed✓ Done£65 / £55 / £100 / £140
UK fulfilment (3PL)PendingNeed UK-based 3PL for domestic shipping. Consider Huboo, Mintsoft, or Amazon FBA UK
UK import / customsPendingConfirm import duty classification for organ supplements (likely zero-rated as food supplement). HMRC check needed.
UK label compliancePendingUK FSA rules for food supplements post-Brexit differ from FDA. Label claims must comply with UK regs.
UK payment processingEasyStripe supports GBP natively. Shopify handles currency switching. Set up GBP storefront.
VAT registrationPendingNeed UK VAT number if selling direct to UK consumers. Register before launch. Accountant task.

What Needs to Happen Now

  • Confirm production lead time with Andrew: How long from order to delivery? If 10+ weeks, the order must be placed by mid-May to hit July launch. This is the single most important question right now.
  • Packaging brief to designer this week: Packaging cost (~$1.20/unit) and design needs to be briefed immediately - lead time on custom pouches is typically 6-8 weeks.
  • UK fulfilment partner: Identify and onboard a UK 3PL before launch. Can't do simultaneous UK launch without it.
  • UK VAT registration: Pass to accountant (Sara Perry, David Owen) immediately. Takes 4-8 weeks.
  • Shopify setup: Multi-currency store (USD + GBP), subscription integration (Recharge or Shopify's native sub), and early access landing page for waitlist.
  • Lead the 3-item bundle in all ads: At $92.75 CM per order, the bundle is where the economics make sense for paid acquisition. Every ad should drive to the 3-item subscribe offer.
