The POAO Problem Explained

Modern Savage Adult Blend contains: Grass Fed Freeze Dried Bovine Liver, Heart, Spleen, Kidney, Lung + Bison Liver, plus Beef Protein Isolate and Bovine Collagen Peptides.

Under UK law (FSA and APHA rules), any supplement containing animal tissues - including freeze-dried organ meats - is classified as a Product of Animal Origin. The FSA specifically lists "meat products: any animal tissues such as thymus glands or ground bone material" as examples requiring POAO import treatment.

What POAO import rules mean in practice:

  • 1. Approved Border Control Post (BCP) - goods must enter through a port with veterinary inspection capability (Felixstowe, Southampton, Tilbury etc.)
  • 2. IPAFFS pre-notification - Animal and Plant Health Agency (APHA) must be notified at least 1 working day before arrival
  • 3. Health certificate - required from the US competent authority (USDA/FDA) for the specific product category
  • 4. Approved establishment list - Lightning Nutra/ACF Pharma's facility must be on APHA's approved list for exporting POAO to the UK. If not listed, the shipment will be stopped at the border.
  • 5. TSE (BSE) regulations - bovine products from USA are subject to Transmissible Spongiform Encephalopathy rules, which may require specific declarations

VAT Registration Options

Bear Witness LLC is a US entity with no UK establishment. Here are the options, fastest first:

Option A - UK Customs Broker as Fiscal Representative (Fastest)

A UK customs broker can act as your Importer of Record and fiscal representative. They import the goods in their name, deal with APHA and VAT, then invoice you the total landed cost. You do not need UK VAT registration for the first shipment. Setup time: days, not weeks.

Best for: July 15 launch. Get goods in, start selling, sort VAT registration in parallel.

Option B - Register Bear Witness LLC for UK VAT (Correct Long-Term Path)

Any non-UK business making taxable supplies in the UK must register for VAT - there is no threshold for overseas businesses. Once registered, you can use Postponed VAT Accounting (PVA) to defer import VAT to your quarterly VAT return, then reclaim it as input VAT if you're selling in the UK (which you are).

Timeline: 4-8 weeks for HMRC to process a non-established taxable person application. Apply via HMRC's VAT1 form now.

Best for: All shipments after the first. Saves ~£11,600+ per 2,500-unit shipment on VAT alone.

Option C - UK Subsidiary / Holding Entity

Incorporate a UK company (Bear Witness Ltd UK or Modern Savage Ltd UK), VAT register it, use it as the importer. More structure, more overhead. Probably overkill for launch volume - revisit when monthly UK sales exceed £10K.
