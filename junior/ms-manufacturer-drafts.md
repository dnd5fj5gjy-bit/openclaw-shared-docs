## FORMULA SPECIFICATION (shared — confidential, under NDA)

**Modern Savage range — three powder blends, 28-serving stand-up pouches.** All animal material grass-fed and freeze-dried (or low-temperature desiccated). Naturally flavoured and lightly sweetened. EU/UK compliant.

**1. Modern Savage (Adult Daily Blend)**
- Primal Organ Blend: grass-fed freeze-dried bovine liver, heart, kidney, lung, spleen, testes + grass-fed freeze-dried bison liver
- Primal Protein Blend: grass-fed bovine collagen peptides + grass-fed beef protein isolate
- Prebiotic fibre + probiotic blend: inulin + live probiotic cultures
- Whole-food vitamin & mineral blend, incl. fermented vitamin K2 (MK-7)
- Fruit blend: nine organic freeze-dried berries/fruits (acai, acerola cherry, blackberry, blueberry, goji, maqui, raspberry, strawberry + more) + organic baobab + organic date powder
- Natural flavour/base: organic honey or maple powder, organic vanilla bean, bamboo extract, Himalayan pink salt

**2. Mini Savage (Kids)**
- Same base, reduced organ set: bovine liver, heart, kidney, spleen + bison liver (no lung, no testes)
- Plus grass-fed bovine colostrum
- Allergen: contains milk

**3. BG Summit Stack (add-on, no organs)**
- CreaVitalis creatine monohydrate
- Electrolyte/hydration blend: freeze-dried coconut water powder, seawater + sea-salt minerals
- NMN, PQQ, trans-resveratrol
- Plant-derived vitamin D3 + K2 (MK-7 from natto)
- L-malic acid, bamboo extract, organic rice hulls; beetroot + pomegranate

*Sourcing note:* bison-liver supply is limited (small herds); where unavailable the adult/kids organ blend can run beef-only, with bison swapped in when supply allows. No pancreas in any product. Reference range: https://www.modernsavage.co
