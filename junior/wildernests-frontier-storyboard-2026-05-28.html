The Frontier

Rough storyboard concept — 28 May 2026
Concept

A lone figure moves through a vast, untouched wilderness. The Wildernests home sits in nature — not above it. Scale and landscape are the hero.

Camera stays behind the figure. Wide shots. The world is immense. The home belongs to it.

Aesthetic & Mood

Frontier. Cinematic. Quiet. Slow burn. The kind of landscape that makes you feel small in the best way.
