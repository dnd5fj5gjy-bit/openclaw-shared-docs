Email Sequence

The 170 signups are your hottest leads. Here's how to convert them.

Send these from a personal Bear address. Not a brand address. Personal email converts 3-5x better for a founder-led brand.

Email 1 — Send today (within 24 hours of signup)

"You're in. Here's what we built."

From Bear personally. Tell the founding story in 3 paragraphs. Why he made this. What it's not (processed, fake, cheap). What it is. Founding tribe discount: 10% off first order. One clear CTA: "Try your first month."

Hook: "When I was on Everest, I wasn't thinking about protein shakes. I was thinking about food. Real food. That's what we've tried to make."
Email 2 — Day 3 (non-openers)

"One question, honestly."

Short. 4 sentences. "Did this land in spam? We're new, so it might have. I wanted to make sure you saw this because I think you'll want to." Resend founding tribe offer with a different subject line.

Subject: "I think this landed in your spam"
Email 3 — Day 7 (openers who haven't purchased)

"This is what's in it. Exactly."

Full ingredient transparency email. Every ingredient, why it's there, where it comes from. Bison organs from North American grass-fed herd. No filler. No binders. This is what separates MS from the £25 protein tub at Holland & Barrett. Founding tribe offer expires in 48 hours.

Hook: "Most supplement companies won't show you the full label. Here's ours."
Email 4 — Day 14 (still not purchased)

"What are you waiting for? (Genuinely asking)"

Direct. Honest. "If the price is the issue, I get it. If you're not sure it'll work for you, here's what I'd say." Address the top 3 objections. Final founding tribe offer — this is the last email at this discount. Move them to a standard nurture after this.

Hook: "I'm not going to pretend this is cheap. Here's why it isn't."
Email 5 — Day 30 (purchasers)

"One month in. What's changed?"

Post-purchase retention email. Ask customers to reply with how they're feeling. Collect testimonials. Remind them of what the 30-day arc looks like biologically. Plant the seed for month 2 renewal.

Hook: "Your gut biome takes 21 days to start shifting. You're almost there."
