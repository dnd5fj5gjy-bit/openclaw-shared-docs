The Pivot

The goal for the next 12 weeks is not sales. It's audience.

Manufacturing is underway. That changes everything. You cannot ship to customers today. So every pound of energy spent trying to convert people to checkout right now is wasted. Instead: spend the next 12 weeks building the biggest, warmest pre-launch audience you can. Then when product arrives in July, you ignite it.

Email Sequence — Pre-Launch

11 emails over 12 weeks. Keep them warm, not annoyed.

The goal of every pre-launch email is the same: make the reader feel like they are part of something, not being sold to. When launch day arrives, they should feel they have been waiting for this.

Week 1 — Send immediately

"You're in. Here's what this is."

Welcome email. Not a pitch. The founding story in 3 paragraphs. Why the family built this. What it isn't. What it is. Thank them for joining early. Tell them they will be first to know when it ships.

Subject: "You're one of the first 200."
Week 2 — Content email

"What's actually in it. Every ingredient."

Full ingredient transparency. Bison organs, why each one. What they do. No BS scientific language — Bear's voice, plain English. Builds conviction without selling.

Subject: "Most supplement brands won't show you this."
Week 4 — Story email

"Why I didn't trust any supplement on the market."

Bear's voice. The problem with the supplement industry. What he was feeding his family before this existed. The moment he decided to make his own. Personal and specific.

Subject: "I read 40 supplement labels last year."
Week 6 — Behind the scenes

"We're 6 weeks into manufacturing. Here's what it looks like."

Photo or description from ACF Pharma. Real manufacturing detail. Makes it tangible. "This is being made right now, for you." Builds anticipation and reality simultaneously.

Subject: "The batch is being made."
Week 8 — Social proof

"What Ollie told me about the formula."

Ollie Matthews as third-party expert validation. His take as a nutritionist on the organ meat blend. Not testimonial-format — conversation format. "I asked our nutritionist to be brutally honest about this."

Subject: "Our nutritionist's honest verdict."
Week 10 — Referral activation

"Give your friends first access."

Activate the referral mechanic. "You're on the early list. Share this link and your friend jumps to the front of the queue with you." Simple, reciprocal, personal.

Subject: "Share this. Your friend gets priority shipping."
Week 11 — Countdown

"Two weeks."

Short email. Very Bear. "We ship in two weeks. I wanted to write this myself. Here's what this has taken." Emotional but not sentimental. Real.

Subject: "Two weeks."
Week 12 — Launch day

"It's here. Founding tribe price — 48 hours only."

The conversion email. Full product range. Founding tribe discount (10% off first order, 48-hour window). Bear's personal sign-off. One clear CTA. Send at 6am EST.

Subject: "We're shipping. Founding price ends Friday."
