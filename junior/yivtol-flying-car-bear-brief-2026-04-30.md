## What YIVTOL Actually Is

Chinese ultralight personal eVTOL company. Based in Shenzhen and Chengdu. Showed at CES 2026 in Las Vegas (January). Their flagship is the **S-ZERO** — single-seat personal aircraft, ducted electric fan design.

**S-ZERO specs (two configs published):**
- Payload: 90 kg (base) / 120 kg (Max)
- Flight time: 30 min / 50 min
- Top speed: 70 km/h / 100 km/h
- Range: 35 km / 60 km
- "Max Version" likely = the 120 kg / 50 min / 60 km variant

**Regulatory status:**
- China: holds China's first ultralight eVTOL permit, deliveries started late 2025
- US: would fly under FAA Part 103 (ultralight, no pilot's licence) — but 103 caps weight, fuel, and speed below S-ZERO's Max numbers. Likely needs base config (or experimental cert) to be legal in US airspace
- UK: CAA has no equivalent of Part 103. Would be unflyable in UK without bespoke permit

**Comparable craft:** Jetson One (Sweden, ~$128k, deliveries 2026-2027), Pivotal BlackFly. Same category — single-seat, sub-kilometre joyride aircraft.
