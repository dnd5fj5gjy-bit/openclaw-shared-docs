## The Pattern (Don't Break This)

Every episode follows the same three-post rhythm:
- **Sunday:** Teaser — hint at the guest, location, or what Bear says happened. No reveal yet.
- **Monday:** Engagement — quote, clip, or question that builds anticipation. Tag the guest account.
- **Tuesday (air day):** Launch post — direct watch prompt. "Tonight. 9pm. Fox Reality." Episode-specific hook.

Fox Reality's carousel collab (when confirmed) should be posted Sunday at 10am. Bear reposts 10-12am window.
