Regulatory

The POAO Compliance Requirements

The freeze-dried bovine liver, heart, spleen, kidney, and lung in the organ blend makes this a Product of Animal Origin. Four things must happen before the shipment can clear UK customs:

  1. GB Approved Establishments List
    Lightning Nutra's Pompano Beach facility must appear on the UK's approved list of US food establishments authorised to export food of animal origin to Great Britain. This list is maintained by APHA (Animal and Plant Health Agency). If they are not listed, the shipment is refused entry. Verification takes one conversation with a specialist broker.
  2. USDA-FSIS Export Health Certificate (EHC)
    Lightning Nutra must obtain a USDA-FSIS EHC for food of animal origin destined for Great Britain. This is issued by a USDA-accredited veterinarian and confirms the product meets UK import requirements. The process typically takes 2-3 weeks. It must be started this week if July 1 is the target.
  3. IPAFFS Pre-Notification
    The importer or their broker must pre-notify APHA via the Import of Products, Animals, Food and Feed System (IPAFFS) at least 24-48 hours before the vessel arrives at Felixstowe. No notification means the shipment is held indefinitely on arrival. The broker handles this as standard.
  4. Border Control Post (BCP) Physical Inspection
    All POAO consignments undergo physical inspection at the BCP. Felixstowe is an authorised BCP for POAO — correct routing. Typical hold time is 1-3 days. Inspection fees run approximately £100-300 per consignment.

FCL vs LCL — Consider This

NTG quoted for a full 20-foot container. At 5,000 units of supplement powder (approximately 3-4 metric tons), you are using around 15-20% of FCL capacity and paying for the rest. LCL (Less than Container Load) could reduce freight cost by 30-50% for this volume. Ask George Baker for an LCL quote before confirming the NTG rate. The trade-off is slightly less scheduling flexibility.
