Their Story

Jamaica,
then everywhere else.

The first set · Greek islands, 2023

They met as family friends in Jamaica. Three years later they were sharing a microphone on a catamaran in Greece - Buster with a fold-up keyboard, Tallulah with a voice that could close a room.

That one evening became the first set. Forty-eight hours in Shrewsbury followed. Then Yellow Door Studios. Then three songs with Sam Grade. Then the Troubadour, twice. Then they moved in together.

Three releases in 2026: Little Lady, Carry Me Home, My Devotion. All Yellow Door. All Sam Grade. One room, two voices, no overdubs.

Now they're just getting started.
