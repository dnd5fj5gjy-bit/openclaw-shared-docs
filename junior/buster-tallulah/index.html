Side Two · the Story

Jamaica,
then everywhere else.

The first set, performed on the boat · Greece, summer 2023
August 2021 · Jamaica

The introduction.

Buster met Tallulah on a family holiday in Jamaica. They were family friends. Nothing happened, except a friendship that lasted long enough to matter later.

Summer 2023 · Greece

The catamaran, and a fold-up keyboard.

Two summers on, both families took a catamaran around the Greek islands together. Beforehand, Buster asked Tilly if she could sing. She said yes. He bought a fold-up keyboard and a portable speaker to the boat.

They practised together, and performed for both families one evening. It was the first set they ever played.

2023 – 2024 · Shrewsbury, London

Forty-eight hours, then a routine.

When the boat tied up, Buster invited Tilly down from London to Shrewsbury. They wrote songs for forty-eight hours straight, made videos, duetted covers. They have not stopped since, trading hometowns whenever they could.

May 2024 · Yellow Door Studios

Their first cut.

They recorded their first song, Little Lady, with producer Sam Grade at Yellow Door Studios. More followed, including Carry Me Home, due for release this year.

August 2024 · Greece, again

The second catamaran.

Same families, same boat, a year later. By the end of the trip, Buster and Tallulah were a couple. A few months after that, Buster moved out of his family home in Shrewsbury and into Tilly's flat in London.

Since · London

The flat, the studio, the songs.

Three releases due this year: Little Lady, Carry Me Home, and My Devotion. All recorded at Yellow Door, all produced by Sam Grade.
