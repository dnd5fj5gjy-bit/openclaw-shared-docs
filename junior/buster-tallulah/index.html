Side Two · the Story

Jamaica, then everywhere else.

They met in Jamaica in August 2021, as family friends. Two summers later, both families took a catamaran around Greece together. Buster brought a fold-up keyboard and a speaker on a hunch. They played their first set for their families, on a boat, off the coast.

Back home they kept writing — Shrewsbury one weekend, London the next. In May 2024 they cut their first song at Yellow Door Studios with producer Sam Grade. By August they were a couple. By winter they were living in London together.

They write standards-shaped songs out of soul, jazz, and a year of long-distance trains. Three of them release this year.
