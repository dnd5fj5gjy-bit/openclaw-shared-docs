## What Goes Wrong (and what to do)

### Stripe isn't processing

**Signal:** Orders not showing in dashboard after Email 5 sends.
**Cause:** Most likely UK card issuer blocking the IOM-registered entity (or Modern Savage Ltd if that's set up). Or Stripe account in review.
**Action:** Calvin calls Stripe support immediately (they have a priority line for launch-day issues). Jesse calls Raemy: is Modern Savage Ltd fully registered and the Stripe entity correct?

### Email deliverability drops

**Signal:** Open rate below 25% on Email 5.
**Cause:** SPF/DKIM/DMARC not fully authenticated on modernsavage.com, or IP reputation (if they've been sending heavy pre-launch volume).
**Action:** Calvin checks Klaviyo's deliverability report. Look for bounces first. If spam rate is above 0.3%, pause sends and fix authentication.

### Ships A Lot isn't dispatching

**Signal:** No order acknowledgment from Susan U by July 1 afternoon.
**Action:** Calvin calls Brian Baskin: 310-403-9335. This is a same-day problem — don't let it run to day 2.

### Bear's post gets negative traction

**Signal:** Comment section running hot with skepticism, price objections, or supplement cynicism.
**Action:** Do not delete comments. Calvin monitors and flags to Jesse. The organic organ supplement community is small and watches for authenticity - engaging honestly with skeptics is better than ignoring them.

### Founding members feel "forgotten" after June 29 purchase

**Signal:** Reply emails from founding members asking "when does this ship?" or "I haven't received anything."
**Action:** OB-1 should have handled this (it tells them when to expect dispatch and what happens next). If they're still confused, Calvin replies personally and flags the gap to Jesse. This is a retention problem in week 1, which is the worst possible time.
