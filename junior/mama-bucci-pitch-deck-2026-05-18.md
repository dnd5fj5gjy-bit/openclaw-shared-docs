## Three deal structures to consider

We've built three options. All three respect Mama Bucci's preference to avoid heavy cash outflow at this growth stage.

### Option A — Performance-aligned (recommended)
- **Cash:** $4,000 / month hard costs (covers production, ad creative, software, ad management)
- **Equity:** Tiered, tied to D2C revenue milestones
  - 2.5% at $1M D2C ARR
  - 2.5% at $5M D2C ARR
  - 2.5% at $10M D2C ARR
- **Term:** 24-month commitment, October launch start
- **Why it works:** Low cash burn. Both parties aligned. BGV only wins big when Mama Bucci wins big.

### Option B — Hybrid retainer + bonus
- **Cash:** $7,500 / month full service
- **Performance bonus:** 5% of net D2C revenue above $1M ARR threshold, capped at $250K/year
- **Term:** 12-month rolling
- **Why it works:** Predictable for both sides. Lower equity dilution. Performance upside still present.

### Option C — Full retainer (no equity)
- **Cash:** $15,000 / month all-in (tech, content, ads, account management)
- **Term:** 12-month rolling
- **Why it works:** If you want pure service with no equity discussion. Comparable to top D2C agencies, with the Bear distribution advantage thrown in.
