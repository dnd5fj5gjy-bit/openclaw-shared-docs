How to Use This

Set this as an automated flow in Klaviyo triggered by signup (waitlist form or site opt-in). Each email builds on the last — trust first, education second, urgency third, proof fourth, offer fifth. Do not send the offer early. The sequence earns the right to ask. Tone throughout: warmly grounded, direct, never clinical. Jesse's voice, not a brand's voice.
