Risk Register
Website not ready on May 1
Mitigation: Confirm wildernests.com launch-readiness with Alex by April 20. If needed, a simple landing page with inquiry form is enough for Day 1 — don't delay the launch for a perfect website.
Bear filming not locked in before April 30
Mitigation: Batch shoot day in the next 2 weeks. Even a remote outdoor shoot works — Bear doesn't need to be physically at a Wildernests structure for the teaser and endorsement content.
Algorithm suppresses organic launch content
Mitigation: Back the launch week with a small paid boost ($500-1K) on Bear's top-performing reel. Targets outdoor/adventure audience in UK and US. Wildernests to confirm budget.
No press coverage despite outreach
Mitigation: Bear Grylls name alone is enough for a news hook. If Dezeen/AD don't bite, The Times and Evening Standard will — human interest angle on UK off-grid housing. Backup: Substack feature from Jesse.
Product-market fit questions surface publicly
Mitigation: Have Alex's FAQ ready for the comments. Key objections: planning permission (Wildernests handles it), price (comparable to a caravan, fraction of bricks and mortar), off-grid viability (fully self-sufficient systems built in).
