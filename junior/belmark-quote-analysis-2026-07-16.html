How the pricing actually works (this matters)

  • Quantities in the quote are total pouches per size, not per SKU. The "Items" rows price how many SKUs share that run: each extra SKU adds only ~$0.01-0.02 per unit, so running all 3 SKUs together costs almost nothing extra.
  • Jesse asked for 2,500 / 5,000 / 10,000 per SKU; Sarah quoted total-quantity tiers of 5,000-30,000 per size. There is no 2,500 tier. "No minimum per item" means a run can be split across SKUs freely, but the lowest priced total per size is 5,000.

The volume sweet spot is 10-15k per size; beyond that the curve flattens (30k only saves another ~9-11%).

Terms and gotchas

  1. Validity 30 July. Prepress only starts after final art files, so either lock the order inside the window or ask Sarah for an extension now.
  2. 1% 10, net 30 - pay within 10 days for the 1% discount; do not pay by card (kills the discount, adds a processing fee).
  3. FOB De Pere, WI - freight to the ACF copacker excluded (US domestic, minor).
  4. Quantity tolerance: billed on what ships, +15%/-10% per Sarah (T&Cs say 10%, with up to 50% overrun possible on small zipper-pouch runs). Budget headroom of ~15% on the invoice.
  5. Possible extras: $300/plate if a varnish/spot plate is needed; "additional graphics charges" confirmed only after art files - ask Sarah to cap or confirm these with the artwork.
  6. T&Cs are standard vendor-favoured boilerplate (Wisconsin law, liability capped at price paid, cancellation right pre-shipment). Not worth negotiating at this order size.

Strategic fit (what changed since 30 Jun)

  • The compostable alternative (Salt of the Earth) failed the moisture-barrier test on 3 Jul (WVTR ~28x worse than PET/foil). Belmark is the launch pouch. This decision is effectively made.
  • The 5k/10k tiers were originally requested to cover the UK order, but UK production is now heading to a UK manufacturer who will source or fill pouches locally. Size this order for US demand only. If the chosen UK partner ends up needing printed pouches supplied, Belmark can quote that volume separately - at 15k+ their cheaper flexo process kicks in.
