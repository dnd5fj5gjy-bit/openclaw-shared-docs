## What actually happened

| Date | Event |
|------|-------|
| Oct 2022 | FDA tells SyncoZymes (NDIN 1247) and Inner Mongolia Kingdomway (NDIN 1259) NMN is excluded from the dietary supplement definition (drug-first issue under 21 USC 321(ff)(3)(B)). |
| 2022–2025 | Industry contests. Natural Products Association files lawsuit. Multiple citizen petitions. |
| Sep 29, 2025 | **FDA reverses.** Two letters confirm NMN is not excluded — it is lawful as a dietary supplement. |
| Dec 2025 | FDA sends confirmation letters to SyncoZymes and Inner Mongolia Kingdomway — NDI status reinstated for both. |
| Jan 2026 | Xiamen Kingdomway Group receives NDI letter for its beta-NMN supplements. |
| **Today** | NMN is legal in US dietary supplements. NDIN premarket notification is still required per supplier (or per company if not sourcing from a filed supplier). |

## What this means for Modern Savage Summit Stack

Two scenarios:

**Scenario A — Lightning Nutra sources from a supplier with an active NDIN.** The three confirmed: SyncoZymes, Inner Mongolia Kingdomway, Xiamen Kingdomway. If Lightning Nutra is using any of these, Modern Savage is covered. No further filing needed. Ship it.

**Scenario B — Lightning Nutra sources from a different supplier without an NDIN.** Then either:
- Lightning Nutra files an NDIN (they may already have one — they're an FDA-registered facility doing this for other clients)
- Modern Savage files an NDIN (~$15-25K legal cost, 75-day FDA review window)
- Or Lightning Nutra switches to a covered supplier (cheapest, fastest)

## Other options (for reference, probably not needed)

- **Switch NMN → NR (nicotinamide riboside):** Same NAD+ precursor mechanism. NR has stronger clinical record (>500 studies, 50+ human trials). Niagen (ChromaDex) is the dominant branded form. Cleaner regulatory record. Slight downside: NR raised whole-blood NAD+ ~25% more than NMN in head-to-head over 2 weeks (so ingredient swap doesn't weaken the product).
- **Drop NAD+ precursor entirely from Summit Stack:** Keep creatine and electrolytes. Lose the longevity angle. Not recommended — NAD+ is core to the Summit Stack story.
