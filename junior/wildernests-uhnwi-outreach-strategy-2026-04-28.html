Wildernests — 500 UHNWI Outreach Strategy

Built: April 28, 2026 Author: Junior For: Jesse Grylls / Alex Aboshiha / Gunnar Trommer — operational launch brief

The Core Challenge

Wildernests' go-to-market is direct outreach to 500 UHNWIs. The problem: UHNWIs at the $10M+ investable asset level don't respond to cold outreach. They screen their email. They have gatekeepers. Family offices exist specifically to filter inbound.

The solution is not a list and a mail merge. The solution is a warm introduction engine — systematically leveraging every relationship in the Bear/BGV/Wildernests orbit to get personal introductions into the right rooms.

Tattarang is the proof of concept. Bear sent one email to one contact at a $20B family office. That's the model. You need to run that play 499 more times.

Who the 500 Should Be

Not all UHNWIs are equal targets. The right Wildernests buyer has a specific profile:

Primary profile (highest conversion likelihood):

  • Net worth: $5M-$50M (not billionaires — they build bespoke, they don't buy modular)
  • Age: 38-58
  • Identity: self-made entrepreneur, tech founder, private equity, senior finance professional
  • Current situation: city primary residence, no satisfying second home, increasingly values "off-grid" escapism
  • Values alignment: environmental consciousness, adventure, physical wellbeing, privacy
  • Geography: UK, US West Coast (LA, SF, Seattle), US East Coast (NYC, Miami), Australia

Secondary profile:

  • Established family wealth (2nd or 3rd generation)
  • Already owns multiple properties — Wildernests is about a new category, not a first second home
  • Conservation and outdoor philanthropy angle

Why not billionaires? Billionaires don't buy product — they commission it. A modular wilderness home at Wildernests' price point is not in that conversation. The $5M-$50M wealth bracket has the money to buy, the desire for something special, and the psychological fit with Bear's aspirational brand.

Bear knows people. Royal connections, military/SAS alumni, celebrity adventurers, media executives, conservation donors. Every person Bear personally introduces to Wildernests converts at a fundamentally different rate than any other channel.

Channel 4: Adventure and Conservation Communities (Brand Alignment)

Wildernests' brand is wilderness, architecture, and escape. The people most pre-disposed to buy are already in communities that share these values.

Specific communities to target:

  • WWF and conservation organisation major donors — people who give £50K+ to conservation charities already have the values alignment and the wealth. Bear is a WWF ambassador and conservation figure — this is a natural connection
  • Yacht and sailing communities — Royal Yachting Association's UHNWI layer, offshore racing community. Jesse sails. Bear sails. This is genuine territory

  • High-end ski/outdoor property owners — people who already bought in Verbier, Courchevel, Aspen are psychologically pre-sold on "escape property" — they're just looking for a new category

How to activate: Targeted outreach from Alex or Gunnar, always with Bear's name in the intro. "Bear invested in this and suggested I reach out..." is the opening. Not cold — warm by association.

Target count from this channel: 60-100 warm introductions. Expected conversation rate: 10-15%. Potential buyers: 6-15.

Channel 5: Curated Digital (Bear's Social Platform)

This is the lowest-conversion channel but the highest reach. Bear's social platform (Instagram, YouTube) reaches millions — but the UHNWI subset within that audience is small. The play here is not mass-market but targeted:

  • Running Wild S9 timing: Each episode airs to a premium audience. Episode guests (MGK, Colman Domingo, Uma Thurman, Elizabeth Banks) have wealthy, adventure-aligned followings. Strategic Wildernests content timed to episode launches can generate inbound from exactly the right demographic
  • The architecture angle: Visually stunning 3D renders and the "Return to Home" video are the organic hook. Content that makes Wildernests feel aspirational and scarce
  • No general advertising: UHNWI targeting via digital advertising is high-cost, low-conversion. This channel works only through Bear's existing platform, not paid acquisition

Target count from this channel: 20-40 genuine inbound leads from the UHNWI tier. Expected conversion: 5-10%. Potential buyers: 1-4.

The Outreach Sequence

Step 1: The Activation Asset (needs to exist before outreach starts)

You need one asset that can travel independently — that a family office principal can forward to their network without Bear making 500 individual introductions.

This is the ~2-minute UHNWI teaser video Gunnar is storyboarding. It needs to:

  • Open with wilderness imagery, not product specs
  • Feature Bear's voice (not just face — voice builds credibility and emotional connection)
  • Show the architecture as a frame for an experience, not as a structure
  • End with scarcity signal: "500 homes. Invitation-only first release."

Calvin's 3D rotation needs to be seen by Jesse and Alex before this video goes further. Jesse's feedback feeds directly into whether the visual language is right.

Step 2: The Personal Intro Letter (Bear's voice)

A single-page letter, handwritten feel, Bear's signature. Not a brochure. Not a pitch deck.

> "I rarely invest in property. I invested in this because I think it will change how the people I respect most experience the wild. If you've ever wanted a home that's genuinely somewhere — not just somewhere nice — I think you should see this. I've asked the team to reach out."

This letter travels ahead of any direct contact. It's the social proof that converts cold to warm.

Step 3: The Initial Contact

From Alex Aboshiha (as CEO), not from Bear directly — Bear's intro was the opener, Alex closes.

  • Email or call (depending on channel)
  • One paragraph: who Bear is, what Wildernests is, one specific reason why this person was chosen (personalisation is critical at this wealth tier)
  • Offer: a private viewing of the materials + a 20-minute conversation with Alex

Step 4: The Private Preview Package

For those who express interest:

  • The 2-minute UHNWI teaser video
  • The 3D architectural renders (from Daniel Aguilar / Calvin's rotation)
  • A one-page summary of investment case and availability
  • No pricing in the first touch — create the conversation

Step 5: The Qualification Call

Alex + Jesse (when he returns May 21). Goal: understand the buyer's specific location preference, use case, timeline. Not a sales call — a discovery call. Builds relationship, qualifies seriousness.

No database, no family office approach, no digital campaign will outperform Bear's personal credibility at this wealth tier. The Tattarang model proves it. The Wildernests launch is not a marketing problem. It's a relationship activation problem.
