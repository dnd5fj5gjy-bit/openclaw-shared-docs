WP·01The Situation

Voice is winning. The voice is anonymous.

ChatGPT Voice already feels like magic. Hundreds of millions of people can ask anything, hands free, in plain speech, and get a brilliant answer back.

But the voice on the other end belongs to no one. It is warm, capable and interchangeable. And when the question really matters, whether you are halfway up a hillside in fog or halfway through a Sunday roast, anonymous puts a ceiling on trust.

For over twenty years, Bear Grylls has been the voice people turn to when things get wild. This proposal is about putting that voice, and voices like it, inside the product the world is already talking to.
