# Modern Savage — General Manager (Operations & Admin)

**Company:** Modern Savage Inc. (Delaware C-Corp, part of the BGV group)
**Reports to:** Jesse Grylls (Founder) and Raemy Singh (CFO)
**Location:** Remote (UK or US), with flexibility across time zones
**Start:** ASAP — launch is mid-September 2026
**Type:** Full-time (open to an initial contract-to-perm arrangement)

---

## The company

Modern Savage is a premium health supplement brand launching in the US in September 2026, with a UK line to follow in late autumn. The hero product is a primal organ blend (bison liver, organ and protein complex) across three SKUs: Adult, Kids and Summit. The brand sits within Bear Grylls Ventures and launches with a warm audience of 55,000+ emails and a founding community already in place.

The formula is locked, the first production run (2,500 units per SKU) is ordered, fulfilment and banking are in place, and the D2C store is being built. What the business needs now is one person who owns the operational spine so the founders can stay on brand, product and growth.

## The role

You are the operational engine of the company. You take ownership of everything between "the founders decided it" and "it actually happened": suppliers, fulfilment, compliance paperwork, customer operations and day-to-day admin. In the first six months this is a hands-on, do-it-yourself role; as the brand scales you build the team and processes around you.

## What you will own

**Supply chain and production**
- Manage the US manufacturer and packaging vendors end to end: specs, quotes, purchase orders, timelines, quality issues
- Chase and close the open items that gate launch (spec sheets, label artwork, packaging print runs)
- Stand up the UK manufacturing track alongside the US one, including supplier onboarding and production scheduling
- Own inventory planning and reorder points across both markets

**Fulfilment and customer operations**
- Run the 3PL relationship (US) and the equivalent UK setup: inbound stock, pick/pack SLAs, shipping issues, returns
- Own customer service from day one: response templates, refunds, subscription changes, escalations
- Monitor subscription health (churn, failed payments, delivery problems) and fix root causes

**Compliance and admin**
- Keep the company compliant on both sides of the Atlantic: FDA labelling and claims (US), FSA/ASA rules (UK), insurance, registrations
- Manage company admin: contracts, invoices, supplier payments, filings, working with the CFO and external lawyers/accountants
- Keep clean records so finance always has accurate numbers on stock, COGS and supplier liabilities

**Launch and general management**
- Run the launch plan as a project: one owner, one timeline, weekly status to the founders
- Coordinate freelancers and agencies (creative, web, marketing) on operational deliverables
- Report weekly on the numbers that matter: units, orders, subscription base, cash committed, blockers

## Who we are looking for

- 4+ years in operations, supply chain or general management at a consumer products company; food, supplement or health brand experience strongly preferred
- Has taken a physical product from purchase order to customer doorstep before, and knows where that process breaks
- Comfortable with US and/or UK supplement compliance, or demonstrably quick to master regulated categories
- Strong administrator: contracts, spreadsheets, supplier negotiation, tidy record-keeping
- Self-managing and decisive: this is a small founding team, not a big-company role with support functions
- Direct communicator who flags problems early and brings options, not just issues

**Nice to have:** experience with subscription D2C (Shopify/Stripe), 3PL management, launching into a second market, or working with founder-led/family-office businesses.

## Package

Competitive salary based on experience, with performance incentives tied to launch and subscription milestones. Details discussed at interview.

## Process

Intro call with the CFO, followed by a working session with the founder. We move quickly for the right person.
