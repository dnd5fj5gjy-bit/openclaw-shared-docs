The Product

Modular wilderness homes.
Built for the extraordinary.

Wildernests designs and deploys modular luxury structures in remote locations — coastlines, forests, mountains. Each home is engineered to be transported and installed with minimal environmental impact. Guests experience true wilderness without compromising on design, comfort, or connectivity.

The Homes

Off-grid modular architecture

  • Fully modular — flat-packed and deployable in 72 hours
  • Solar + battery powered; zero grid dependency
  • Premium interiors: hardwood, stone, architect-designed
  • Located in exclusive wilderness sites — not serviced land
  • Minimum environmental footprint; no permanent foundations
  • Sleeps 2-6; private chef and concierge available on request

The Model

Revenue and ownership structure

  • Homes sold to landowners / investors; Wildernests operates
  • Revenue split: booking income shared with home owners
  • Wildernests retains operational margin and brand licensing
  • Repeat deployment model — same home, multiple locations
  • Platform play: software and booking infrastructure scales
  • Target ADR: £1,200-£2,800 per night per unit

Competitive Advantages

Why Wildernests wins.

  • 🐻

    Bear Grylls — Unmatched Distribution

    Bear Grylls has 100M+ social followers across Instagram, YouTube, and TikTok. His audience is precisely the profile of a Wildernests guest: affluent, adventure-oriented, nature-connected. No luxury hospitality brand in the world has this kind of earned distribution. Wildernests launches with it built in.

  • 🏗

    Modular Design — Speed and Scalability

    Traditional luxury hospitality takes 3-5 years to develop a site. Wildernests deploys in 72 hours. This creates a scalability advantage no conventional competitor can replicate. Each new location is an iteration on a proven template, not a ground-up build.

  • 🌍

    Location Access — The Untouched Wilderness

    Wildernests targets sites inaccessible to conventional construction. Cliff edges, forest clearings, mountain plateaus. The modular design unlocks locations that no hotel could reach — creating a product category that cannot be commoditised by scale players.

  • ♻️

    ESG Credentials — The Sustainability Imperative

    No permanent foundations. Solar powered. Minimal waste footprint. At a time when HNW travellers face increasing social pressure around travel choices, Wildernests offers genuine wilderness luxury with genuine environmental credentials.
