# Wildernests — Intro Film Script
## Variation 2 | 2:00 | "A New Way to Live"

**Tone:** Emotional narrative arc. Tension to revelation to aspiration.  
**Music:** Ambient electronic with orchestral elements. Tension in the first third, opens in the second, soars in the third.

---

| Timecode | Scene | VO / On-Screen |
|---|---|---|
| 0:00–0:12 | Stock aerial: vast untouched wilderness. No roads. No infrastructure. Music starts immediately — textured, moving. | *"Every generation inherits a different version of the world."* |
| 0:12–0:30 | Stock montage: city traffic, power lines cutting through a skyline, suburban street at rush hour. Fast cuts, 3–4 seconds each. | *"Ours inherited a world built on grids. On systems. On infrastructure that ultimately has the final say."* |
| 0:30–0:45 | Cut. Aerial stock of raw, extraordinary land: Scottish loch, Patagonian coast, red rock plateau. Music lifts. The landscape opens up. | *"But is that how we were meant to live."* |
| 0:45–1:05 | The home enters. Renders in three locations — cliff, forest clearing, desert mesa. Each held for 6 seconds. Music driving. | *"Or are we meant to live in harmony with the world. Symbiotically. Self-sufficiently. Wildernests was built to give you your freedom back. To be the final evolution of civilisation. The world's first fully autonomous home. Fully off-grid. Your home generates its own water and power. Assembled in 72 hours."* |
| 1:05–1:25 | Slower sequence. Home at night — lit from within, dark landscape surrounding it. Then at dawn. Then in rain — standing still while the weather moves around it. | *"Built to last a generation. Built to withstand what the land demands. Built for people who refuse to let a grid decide where they live."* **On-screen:** Fire resistant. Flood and hurricane safe. |
| 1:25–1:45 | Final render sequence. Wide shots. Home in four landscapes, each more dramatic than the last. Music builds to its fullest. | *"This is not a home. This is the next generation of human living."* |
| 1:45–2:00 | Music pulls back. One last wide render — home on a cliff, ocean stretching to the horizon. Pull back slowly. Wildernests wordmark fades in. | *"How will you live?"* Fade to black. |
