Channel Strategy

Instagram
Primary — Brand & Social Proof
@modernsavage handle. Aesthetic: dark, raw, outdoor. Bear content + user UGC + product shots in the field.

KPI: 50K followers by end of launch month. 3-5% engagement rate.
Paid: Retargeting only — don't buy followers. Let organic do the work first.
Key move: Bear reposts to his @beargrylls (20M+ followers) on launch day.
TikTok
Growth engine — discovery & virality
Short-form. Lead with "does it work?" not "here's our brand story." Hooks: "I tried eating only organs for 30 days," "The supplement Bear Grylls actually takes," survival nutrition content.

KPI: 1 video per day launch week. One video breaks 1M views.
Key move: Seed 20 TikTok creators in adventure/biohacking space 4 weeks pre-launch. No paid partnership — gift product, let them post organically.
YouTube
Long-form trust builder
Dedicated channel or integrated with Bear's existing YouTube. 10-20 minute deep dives: organ nutrition science, sourcing story (grass-fed NZ/AU), survival nutrition explainers.

KPI: Launch video hits 500K views in month 1 via Bear channel amplification.
Key move: Get onto Huberman Lab, Joe Rogan, or equivalent in months 2-3.
Podcast / Audio
Consideration — converts serious buyers
Target: survival/outdoor podcasts, biohacking shows, carnivore community. Bear as guest, not ad-read. Guest spots convert far better than pre-roll ads.

KPI: 5 significant podcast appearances in first 90 days.
Key move: Rogan appearance is the "nuclear option." Pitch via EBG connection. One Rogan appearance = 6-figure revenue day.
DTC Website (Shopify)
Revenue engine — own the customer
Subscription-first. Default to subscribe & save on checkout (30% off). Email/SMS capture before launch via waitlist — target 10K sign-ups. Launch day email to waitlist converts at 8-15%.

KPI: 40%+ subscribers by month 3. AOV $65+.
Key move: Launch waitlist NOW. Every day without one is a day of lost emails.
Amazon
Secondary — credibility & discovery
List on Amazon from day 1 but don't optimise for it early. Amazon reviews will build naturally. Position premium above Ancestral Supplements. Do NOT race them on price.

KPI: 4.5+ star rating. 100 reviews by month 3.
Key move: Email DTC customers to leave Amazon reviews. Drives algorithm ranking fast.

Core Messaging — What to Say and How

Headline

"Nutrition your body actually recognises."

One-liner

"Modern Savage packs what your ancestors ate daily — liver, heart, kidney — into one capsule. No synthetics. No shortcuts. Just concentrated nutrition from grass-fed animals."

Bear's voice (first person)

"I've eaten organs in the wild on every continent. Not because I had to — because I know what real fuel feels like. Modern Savage is that, simplified."

What to avoid

Avoid clinical language (bioavailability, mitochondrial, peptide chains). Your competitor Heart & Soil already owns that angle — and it limits mass appeal. Own the instinct angle, not the science angle. "Your body knows what this is" beats "clinical dose of CoQ10."
