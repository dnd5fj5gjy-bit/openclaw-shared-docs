## WVTR — WHAT TO VERIFY IN THE PDF

From Melissa's tech sheet, check:

1. **WVTR value** (Water Vapour Transmission Rate) — supplement-grade powders need <1.0 g/m²/day. Acceptable range for compostable with barrier coating: 0.2-0.5 g/m²/day. If she doesn't quote it, ask explicitly.

2. **OTR value** (Oxygen Transmission Rate) — organ powders oxidise if not properly sealed. Look for <50 cc/m²/day.

3. **Nitrogen-flush compatibility** — confirm the pouch seal integrity holds at Lightning Nutra's filling line pressure.

4. **Shelf life data for powdered nutraceuticals** — any customer reference from supplement or superfood brands.
