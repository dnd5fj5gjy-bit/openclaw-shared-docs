Risk Flags

Four risks that could delay or damage the launch if not addressed now.

High Risk
Trademark Not Filed
No trademark on "Modern Savage" means a competitor can file first and block the launch. US and UK filings should happen this week - not after the launch campaign builds awareness.
High Risk
Formula Still Unlocked
If ACF Pharma pricing or formula isn't confirmed this week, the production timeline compresses. 90 days is already tight - every week of delay pushes launch toward August.
Medium Risk
No Email List at Launch
Without a waitlist of 1,000+ built before launch, Day 1 relies entirely on paid ads. Email converts 3-5x better than cold traffic. Start capturing emails now.
Medium Risk
FDA Label Compliance
Organ blend supplements have specific FDA rules on health claims. Review all planned label copy with ACF Pharma before packaging brief goes to designer. "Supports family nutrition" - fine. "Boosts immunity" - regulated.
