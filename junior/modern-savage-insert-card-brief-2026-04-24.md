**Prepared:** April 24, 2026
**Author:** Junior
**For:** Calvin / design team
**Cost:** $0.15 per order (Ships A Lot confirmed)
**Purpose:** Every order ships with an insert card. At $0.15 it costs virtually nothing and does four things no email can: it's physical, it arrives at the moment of excitement (first delivery), it reinforces the identity purchase, and it drives referrals at zero additional CAC.

---

## What a Good Insert Card Does

1. **Confirms the decision.** The buyer is holding the product for the first time. They might feel buyer's remorse. The card removes it.
2. **Sets expectations.** Week 1 looks different from Month 2. Tell them what to expect so they don't quit at Day 5.
3. **Drives the referral.** The moment the box is opened is the highest-enthusiasm moment in the entire customer lifecycle. That's when to ask.
4. **Gives Bear's voice one more touch point.** Physical Bear = different from email Bear.

## Card Dimensions and Format

Standard insert: 4"×6" (10×15cm). Double-sided. Card stock, not paper — feels premium without adding cost.

- **Front:** Brand identity + Bear
- **Back:** Practical content (how to take, referral, QR code)

Ships A Lot confirmed they can insert this at $0.15/unit. Print cost for 4×6 card stock at 500+ units is roughly $0.08-0.12/card. Total cost per order: ~$0.23-0.27. At $65/order that's 0.4% of revenue. Spend it.

## What NOT to Put on the Insert

- Ingredients list — they can find this online
- QR code to Instagram — premature ask before product arrives
- Review request — too early (first delivery, before they've tried it)
- Discount codes — trains customers to expect discounts; wrong signal for this brand
- Long body copy — they won't read it
- "Thank you for your purchase" — corporate, not Bear

---

## Sequencing: Change the Card Over Time

**Founding Member card (first 170 customers):**
Add a small additional line on the back: *"Founding Member — one of [X] people who believed in this first."* Print these separately. The 170 founding customers deserve something slightly different.

**Standard card (Month 2 onward):**
The version described above. Can be reprinted as the referral program evolves.

**Month 3+ card:**
Add a "90-day milestone" callout: *"Three months in? Tell us your story. [QR to short form]"* — feeds the user-generated content engine for future social proof.
