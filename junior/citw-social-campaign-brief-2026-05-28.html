Audience

Who is the Chosen audience?

Understanding this audience is the key to running the social campaign well. The Chosen is the world's most-funded crowdfunded media project ever - raised over $40M directly from fans before a single episode aired. It has 180+ million viewers across 190 countries. The fanbase is unlike anything in mainstream TV.

✝

Faith-Driven

Deeply Christian. The show is a retelling of the life of Jesus. Audience chose to fund it - not passive consumers but active believers.

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦

Family-Oriented

Multi-generational viewing. Parents and children watch together. Family is core identity. This is not a youth audience - it skews 25-55.

💬

Extraordinarily Engaged

Word-of-mouth driven. The Chosen grew entirely through organic sharing. When The Chosen audience finds something they love, they tell everyone.

🌍

Global + US-Heavy

Strong in US evangelical communities, but global reach. US, UK, Latin America, East Africa. English-dominant but culturally wide.

Why this audience is Bear's ideal

Bear Grylls' faith is authentic and well-known. His public Christianity - the survival, the family, the service - resonates precisely because The Chosen audience can see it's real. They're not meeting Bear the TV star. They're meeting Bear the Christian father and outdoorsman.

Jesse's messaging framing: Faith, family, and the wild. These are the things that matter. Bear lives them every day.

  • Bear's survival content maps to their values of self-reliance, stewardship of creation, and provision for family
  • The Grylls family dynamic (Bear, Jesse, the boys) resonates with their family-first worldview
  • Modern Savage positions directly into "feed your family right" - this audience buys it

Strategic Angle

The Modern Savage cross-promotion play

This is the opportunity most people would miss. The Chosen audience and Modern Savage's target audience are the same people. Both launch in the same summer window. Running a gentle bridge between them is worth real money.

👥

Same Audience

Faith-driven, family-oriented Christian adults aged 25-55. Modern Savage targets "families and health-conscious adults." These are the same people.

📅

Perfect Timing

Modern Savage launches July 1. Bear's CITW appearance is July 9. The audience that discovers Modern Savage through the Bear launch will see Bear again in CITW one week later.

🌿

Values Alignment

Modern Savage's "feed your family right" mission maps exactly to The Chosen audience's family stewardship values. Primal, whole food, no shortcuts. This is their language.

📧

Email + Community

The Founding Tribe email list being built now (Bear sent Email 1 May 26) - this audience will overlap heavily with CITW viewers. One list, two activations.

How to execute the bridge

Don't make it obvious. Don't say "buy Modern Savage because I'm in Chosen In The Wild." Let the values do the work:

  • In CITW content: Bear's identity as a father, protector, man of faith - the same values Modern Savage sells on. No need to mention Modern Savage explicitly until it launches.
  • After July 1 launch: In CITW social posts, Bear can naturally reference "what I've been feeding my family this summer." One-line, no hard sell.
  • Email list connection: July Founding Tribe email can reference CITW as a moment - "Bear has been on a big summer. Here's what we've been building."
  • Jesse's personal channels: Jesse can make this connection explicitly. "The family values in Chosen In The Wild are the same ones behind Modern Savage." More authentic from the son than from Bear.
