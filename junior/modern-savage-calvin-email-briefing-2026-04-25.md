## The Setup

170 people signed up to the Modern Savage waitlist. They've been waiting. Once Jesse generates the Stripe Payment Link (Tuesday April 28), a 4-email sequence goes out over 7 days.

**Sender name:** Bear Grylls (or "Bear Grylls, Modern Savage")
**From address:** hello@modernsavage.com
**Format:** Plain text or minimal HTML only. No branded templates. The intimacy of plain text is the point.
**Platform:** Klaviyo (recommended) or whatever ESP Calvin is already using for BG content.

---

## The 4 Emails

### Email 1 — "I owe you an update."
**Send:** Day 0. As soon as the Stripe Payment Link exists. Do not wait.
**Preview:** `You signed up a while ago. Here's where things stand — and what it means for you.`
**Goal:** Re-engage the list. Tell Bear's origin story. Ask for a "yes" reply. Set up founding member framing.
**CTA:** Reply with "yes" if you're still in.
**Full brief:** `modern-savage-email-1-2026-04-24.md`

**One action before sending:** Set up a segment/tag called "Founding Member — High Intent" that captures everyone who replies "yes." This segment gets priority treatment at launch.

**A/B test:** "I owe you an update." vs "Still with us?" — pick winner at 4-hour open rate, send to remainder.

---

### Email 2 — "What's actually in it."
**Send:** Day 3 (3 days after Email 1)
**Preview:** `A lot of supplement brands won't answer this question directly. We will.`
**Goal:** Deliver the proof. Ingredients (liver, heart, kidney), sourcing (NZ/Australia, grass-fed, freeze-dried), the kids range. Invite reply questions to feed Email 3.
**CTA:** Soft — "more soon." P.S. asks readers to reply with their question.
**Full brief:** `modern-savage-email-2-proof-2026-04-25.md`

**One action:** Save any replies that come in. The 2-3 most common questions become Email 3 content. This makes the launch email feel responsive and organic.

---

### Email 3 — "You're in. Here's how to lock it in."
**Send:** Day 7 (7 days after Email 1)
**Preview:** `Founding member pricing opens tomorrow. This is what it includes and how it works.`
**Goal:** Convert. Short email. $69/month stated plainly. Stripe link appears twice. No fake scarcity — "there are 170 of you and we can handle that." Founding price is real, it closes when Jesse decides (Jesse: please confirm the end date — suggested: 500 subscribers or July 1, then price goes to $79/month).
**Full brief:** `modern-savage-email-3-launch-2026-04-25.md`

**ACTION REQUIRED — insert before sending:**
Replace `[Claim your founding member spot →]` with the actual Stripe Payment Link.

---

### Onboarding Email — "You're in. Here's what happens next."
**Send:** Automatically, immediately on Stripe subscription confirmed (Klaviyo trigger: "Subscription Created" event, or equivalent)
**Preview:** `Your founding member subscription is confirmed. A few things worth knowing before it arrives.`
**Goal:** Validate the decision. Set expectations (shipping timeline). Invite to WhatsApp Community. How to take the capsules. Referral link.
**Full brief:** `modern-savage-onboarding-email-2026-04-25.md`

**ACTIONS REQUIRED before launch:**
1. **WhatsApp Community link** — Jesse or Calvin creates a WhatsApp Community called "MODERN SAVAGE Founding Members" and generates an invite link. Insert as `[LINK]` in the email.
2. **Referral link** — if using ReferralCandy or similar, generate personalised links per customer. If using manual codes, create simple codes (e.g. FOUNDER01 through FOUNDER170) and map them to subscribers. Insert as `[REFERRAL LINK]`.
3. **Shipping timeline** — confirm with Ships A Lot (Susan U): how many business days from order to shipment? Insert as `[within X business days]`.
