## VO DIRECTION

- Tone: calm authority, not alarming
- Not salesy — matter-of-fact, like a friend who happens to be a doctor
- British accent preferred
- Pace: measured, slight pause after "They say your levels are *normal*"

---

## PRODUCTION NOTES FOR YUSTUDIO

- Motion graphics only (no stock footage of men looking tired)
- Colour palette: Ted's Health navy/teal — professional medical feel without being cold
- Text animations: clean, minimal — similar to the Sentient Desk countdown style
- Music: low-key ambient underscore, builds slightly into the CTA
