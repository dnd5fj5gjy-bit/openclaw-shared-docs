The Bigger Picture

Why FOX Is a Genuine Upgrade

Nat Geo is a prestige cable channel with a loyal but niche audience. FOX is broad broadcast television — the same network as the NFL, American Idol, and Family Guy. The audience Bear gets on FOX on a Tuesday night is larger, younger (relative to cable), and far more commercially valuable. If the show performs, it opens doors: more seasons, bigger guests, broader sponsorship conversations, and a platform for any BGV product that Bear can credibly mention. This is the biggest mainstream moment BGV has had in years — arguably since Bear's NBC peak. Treat it accordingly.
