Modern Savage — Ad Creative Playbook

6 ready-to-shoot concepts · Built on competitor VOC research · Brief to creator or shoot direct

Why these angles work

The gap in the market

AG1 owns "morning routine / replace your stack." Ancestral owns "ancestral, carnivore, primal." Neither owns family-first, full-transparency premium — which is exactly what Modern Savage is. Every ad concept below attacks from that position.

The #1 objection to solve in every ad

"I've tried supplements before and didn't notice anything." VOC research shows this kills conversions more than price. Lead with specificity and proof mechanics (real ingredients, sourcing, no proprietary blends) rather than outcome claims.

Bear's story is the unfair advantage — use it

Competitors pay millions for celebrity associations. Bear built this himself, for his family. That's not a marketing angle — it's a founding story. It creates authentic differentiation no ad budget can replicate.

Every ingredient is listed. Every source is known. No fillers. No blends we can't explain. If it's not good enough for my kids, it's not in the formula."
