New opportunities (discovered this audit)

💡 Mei Wan (dietitian) is an underused asset

Ted's now has a specialist dietitian focused on weight loss and type 2 diabetes remission. This unlocks a content angle Arc TRT doesn't have: TRT as a metabolic health intervention, not just a "low T" fix. A single /trt-and-weight-loss page targeting that keyword cluster could be genuinely differentiated.

💡 ED pricing already competitive — needs a comparison page

Tadalafil from £1.63/pill is competitive with Manual, Bluechew, and LloydsPharmacy Online. Ted's isn't positioning this. A /compare-ed-treatments page with a transparent price table vs competitors would capture high-intent "cheapest tadalafil UK" searches and convert on price + trust simultaneously.

💡 "Optimise" supplement bridges the cold-to-TRT funnel

Optimise targets men not ready for TRT. That's the largest addressable audience for a TRT clinic — men who know something's wrong but aren't sure TRT is the answer. A dedicated landing page for Optimise, positioned as "start here," could function as a low-friction entry point that feeds the TRT conversion pipeline 3-6 months later.
