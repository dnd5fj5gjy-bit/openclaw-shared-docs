# Felix Nightly Surprise — 2026-04-03

## Tiny win
A recurring nightly surprise is a good fit for a **2-minute daily reset** instead of an open-ended todo.

### Suggested routine
1. Check calendar for the next day.
2. Scan inbox/Telegram for anything that needs a reply.
3. Pick the single highest-leverage action for tomorrow.
4. Write it in one line.

### Copy-paste prompt
> What is the one thing Felix should do tomorrow that would make the rest of the day easier?

### Why this helps
- reduces morning context switching
- turns vague pressure into one clear next action
- keeps the system pointed at leverage, not noise
