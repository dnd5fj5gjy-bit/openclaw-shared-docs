Common Questions
Do I need to be an athlete to benefit from this?
No. Creatine benefits cognitive function as much as muscle performance — the research on brain health is significant. NMN and PQQ are relevant to anyone interested in cellular health as they age. What's the difference between NMN and NR (nicotinamide riboside)?
Both are NAD+ precursors. NMN is one step closer to NAD+ in the biosynthetic pathway. The research comparing them directly is still developing — both have legitimate mechanistic support. Modern Savage uses NMN. If you're currently taking NR and seeing results, there's no strong reason to switch — but NMN is the current preference in the longevity research community, which is why it's in the Summit Stack.

Yes — technically. The Summit Stack is a complete product. But it was designed to layer on top of Modern Savage's whole-food nutritional foundation. The performance and longevity compounds work better when the underlying nutritional base is solid.
