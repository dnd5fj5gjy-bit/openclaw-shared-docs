# Felix Nightly Surprise: 3-line shutdown checklist

Tiny thing, but high leverage: a frictionless end-of-day checklist that catches the stuff that costs tomorrow morning.

## The list
1. Close loops: send/queue anything that’s still mentally open.
2. Seed tomorrow: write the first task for the morning in one sentence.
3. Protect energy: leave the desk physically reset, charger on, water ready.

## Why this helps
- Reduces morning activation energy.
- Makes “what do I do first?” disappear.
- Converts vague guilt into one concrete next step.

## Optional upgrade
If Felix wants, turn this into a one-click daily reminder card or a Telegram inline checklist.
